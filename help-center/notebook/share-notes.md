# How to Share Your Notes

Easily share your trading notes with colleagues, mentors, or team members using TradeZella's sharing feature.

## Sharing a Note

Follow these simple steps to share any note:

### Step 1: Locate Your Note

1. Navigate to the note you want to share
2. Look for the **three dots** icon in the top right corner of the note

### Step 2: Generate Share Link

1. Click the **three dots** menu
2. Select **"Share"** from the dropdown options
3. The system generates a shareable link for your note

### Step 3: Share with Others

1. **Copy the link** to your clipboard
2. Click **"Continue"** to confirm
3. Share the link with anyone via email, chat, or messaging platform

## Who Can Access Your Notes

Once you share a note via link, anyone with that link can access it. You have full control over which notes are shared and can manage sharing at any time.
