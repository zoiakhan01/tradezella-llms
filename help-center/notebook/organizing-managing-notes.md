# Organizing and Managing Your Notes Within Your TradeZella Notebook

Master the tools and techniques for keeping your trading notes structured and accessible.

## Adding Notes

You can add notes from multiple sources within TradeZella:

- Add directly from Daily Journal entries
- Create notes from Trade Tracking sessions
- Start fresh in the Notebook section
- Auto-sync to your custom folders for instant organization

## Managing Your Notes

TradeZella provides flexible management options for your notes:

### Deletion and Recovery

Notes moved to Recently Deleted folder can be recovered, allowing you to safely clean up without permanent loss.

### Sharing and Access

- **Share via links** - Generate shareable links to specific notes
- **Download as PDF** - Export notes for external use or archival

### Organization Tools

- **Tags** for flexible filtering and categorization
- **Custom folders** for hierarchical organization
- **Note templates** for consistent formatting across similar notes

## Best Practices

Implement these strategies to maximize your notebook effectiveness:

- Schedule regular trade recaps to capture key learnings
- Maintain a dedicated Goals folder for tracking objectives
- Create Daily Journal templates for consistent journaling
- Use tags to cross-reference related trades and setups
