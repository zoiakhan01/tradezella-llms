# Reports: Options

The Options report in TradeZella helps options traders analyze their performance based on Days Till Expiration (DTE) — the number of days until an option contract expires.

## What This Report Shows

### Trade Distribution by Days Till Expiration

Reflects the total number of options trades with the corresponding Days Till Expiration. For example, you might see that you took 13 trades with a 2 DTE, where the contract was set to expire 2 days from the trade entry date.

### Performance by Days Till Expiration

Reflects the total performance, or total P&L, for trades taken with a specified DTE. This helps you see whether trades with closer or further-out expirations tend to be more profitable.

## Key Insights

This report helps you evaluate whether you perform better under specific trade criteria. For example, you can determine if you have a better return when taking trades with further-out expirations versus same-day expirations. This data-driven analysis helps you refine your options strategy by focusing on the DTE ranges that historically produce your best results.
