# How to Use Filters in TradeZella

## Overview

Filters are available across multiple sections of TradeZella including Dashboard, Trade Log, Reports, and Daily Journal, enabling granular analysis of your trading data.

## Available Filtering Options

TradeZella provides comprehensive filtering capabilities across multiple dimensions:

## Date Range

Filter trades by specific time periods to analyze performance over custom intervals.

## Account

Isolate analysis to specific trading accounts when managing multiple accounts.

## Strategy

Examine performance of individual strategies in isolation.

## Tags

Filter by custom tags you've applied to trades for flexible categorization.

## Additional Filters

- Symbol - Filter by specific trading instruments
- Side - Separate long and short trades
- Trade Result - View winners, losers, or breakeven trades
- Duration - Filter by trade holding periods

## Combining Filters

## Granular Analysis

Stack multiple filters together to create highly specific views of your trading data, enabling deeper insights into performance patterns across various dimensions.
