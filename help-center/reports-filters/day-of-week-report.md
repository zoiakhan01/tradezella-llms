# Understanding TradeZella Day of Week Report

## Overview

The Day of Week Report breaks down your trading performance across Monday through Friday, helping you identify which days are most conducive to profitable trading.

## Performance Metrics by Day

Each day of the week displays key performance indicators:

## Monday through Friday Analysis

- Net P&L - Daily profit or loss
- Trade Count - Number of trades executed on each day
- Win Rate - Percentage of winning trades per day
- Profit Factor - Ratio of winning trades to losing trades by day

## Identifying Trading Patterns

## Most Profitable Days

Discover which days historically generate the strongest returns, allowing you to adjust your strategy focus accordingly.

## Least Profitable Days

Identify underperforming days where you might reduce trading activity or apply stricter risk management protocols.
