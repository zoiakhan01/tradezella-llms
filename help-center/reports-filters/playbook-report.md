# Reports: Playbook

TradeZella's Reports: Playbook section helps you assess how your trading strategies (playbooks) contribute to your overall performance. By breaking down results by playbook, you can identify which approaches drive success, where adjustments are needed, and how to allocate your focus and capital more effectively. Access the Playbook report from the Reports menu in your TradeZella dashboard.

## What You'll See

### Performance Summary

A snapshot showing your best and worst performing playbooks (by net P&L), most active playbooks (most frequently used), and highest win rate playbooks. Quickly spot which strategies contribute most to your success or need refinement.

### Customizable Charts

Interactive charts to visualize performance across playbooks. Plot up to three metrics side-by-side (e.g., Net P&L, Avg Drawdown, Trade Count) using bar or line charts. Available metrics include profitability (Net P&L, profit factor, trade expectancy, avg win/loss), risk and drawdown (avg planned/realized R-Multiple, max drawdown), trading activity (trade count, volume, logged days), streaks and consistency (win %, consecutive wins/losses), and time analysis (avg hold time, trade duration).

### Summary Table

A detailed table listing performance metrics for each playbook. Includes risk and profitability metrics, activity and volume breakdown, and streak data. Sort by any column header to identify your best and worst performing playbooks at a glance.

### Cross Analysis

Apply a secondary filter to break down performance within each playbook. Cross-filter by account, tag, symbol, day and time, entry/exit time intervals, position size, or R-Multiple. For example, compare playbook performance across different symbols or trading hours to uncover hidden patterns.

## Example Use Cases

- **Identifying Top Performers:** Spot which playbook accounts for most of your profits and focus more on that strategy.
- **Spotting Inconsistencies:** Notice a playbook with good profit factor but low win rate and review setups for improvement.
- **Cross-Analyzing by Symbol:** Find which playbooks perform best on specific symbols and adjust your approach accordingly.
- **Comparing Across Timeframes:** See which playbooks work best at different times of day or trade durations.
