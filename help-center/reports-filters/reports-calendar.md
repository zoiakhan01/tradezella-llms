# Reports - Calendar

The Calendar Report in TradeZella provides a visual overview of your trading performance across days, weeks, and months. This heat map style view helps you quickly identify your most and least profitable trading days.

## Accessing the Calendar Report

To view your calendar report:

1. Log in to your TradeZella account
2. Navigate to "Reports" in the main menu
3. Click on "Calendar" or "Calendar View"
4. Select your preferred time period
5. The calendar will display with color coding

## Understanding Calendar Color Coding

Calendar cells are color-coded to represent performance:

- **Green**: Profitable trading days (positive P&L)
- **Red**: Losing trading days (negative P&L)
- **Gray**: Days with no trades
- **Light Green/Red**: Smaller profits/losses
- **Dark Green/Red**: Larger profits/losses
- **Intensity**: Darker colors indicate larger P&L values

The color intensity provides immediate visual feedback on your performance.

## What the Calendar Shows

Each calendar cell displays key metrics:

- **Date**: The day of the trading month
- **P&L**: Daily profit or loss amount
- **Win Rate**: Percentage of winning trades that day
- **Trade Count**: Total number of trades executed
- **Color**: Visual representation of profitability

Click on any day to see detailed breakdown.

- Compare performance across months
- Navigate between months easily

### Weekly View

- See daily performance progression
- Analyze trading patterns within the week

### Yearly View

- Overview of annual performance
- Spot seasonal trading patterns
- Compare performance across months

## Color-Coded Performance Levels

Different shades represent performance tiers:

- **Exceptional**: Large profits (darkest green)
- **Strong**: Good profits (medium green)
- **Break-even/Small**: Minor profits (light green)
- **Small Loss**: Small losses (light red)
- **Moderate Loss**: Medium losses (medium red)
- **Significant Loss**: Large losses (darkest red)

## Customization Options

Customize your calendar report:

1. Click "Customize" or the settings icon
2. Select metrics to display:
   - P&L amount
   - Win rate percentage
   - Trade count
   - Average trade profit
   - Largest win/loss
3. Choose color scheme preferences
4. Set date range filters
5. Apply strategy or instrument filters
6. Save your custom view

## Filtering Data

Use filters to focus on specific data:

- **Date Range**: Custom time period selection
- **Strategy**: Show results for specific strategies
- **Instrument**: Filter by currency pair, stock, or symbol
- **Trade Type**: Long only, short only, or all trades
- **Performance Level**: Show only profitable/losing days
- **Account**: Select specific trading account

## Interactive Features

Calendar report includes interactive elements:

- **Hover**: See tooltip with detailed metrics
- **Click**: Drill down into day's specific trades
- **Drag**: Select multiple days for comparison
- **Zoom**: Expand or contract calendar view
- **Export**: Save calendar as image or PDF

## Day-Level Analysis

Click on any day to see:

- **All Trades**: Complete list of that day's trades
- **Performance Metrics**: Win rate, P&L, trade count
- **Detailed Breakdown**: Entry/exit prices, durations
- **Strategy Performance**: Results by strategy that day
- **Instrument Performance**: Results by trading instrument
- **Time Distribution**: When you entered trades

## Exporting Calendar Data

Export your calendar report:

1. Click "Export" or the download icon
2. Choose format (PDF, image, CSV)
3. Select date range to export
4. Customize display options
5. Download the file

## Comparing Multiple Periods

View multiple months side-by-side:

1. Select "Compare Periods"
2. Choose two or more time periods
3. View calendars in parallel
4. Analyze performance differences
5. Identify improvement areas
