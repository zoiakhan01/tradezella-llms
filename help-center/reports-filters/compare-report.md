# Understanding the Compare Report in TradeZella

## Overview

The Compare Report allows you to evaluate multiple trading segments side by side, providing direct comparisons that illuminate performance differences and guide strategic decisions.

## Comparison Dimensions

## Multiple Segment Types

Compare different aspects of your trading:

- Strategies - Evaluate performance of different trading strategies against each other
- Symbols - Compare how your methods perform across different trading instruments
- Time Periods - Analyze performance trends over different timeframes
- Tags - Compare custom-tagged groups of trades
- Accounts - Evaluate performance across multiple trading accounts

## Key Metrics in Comparison View

## Performance Comparison

View critical metrics side by side for all selected segments:

- Net P&L comparison
- Win rate across segments
- Profit factor ratios
- Trade count per segment
- Average win/loss amounts

## Data-Driven Decision Making

## Strategic Focus

Use comparative insights to determine which strategies, symbols, or time periods merit increased focus and capital allocation.

## Resource Allocation

Identify underperforming segments where you might reduce activity or implement additional risk management protocols.
