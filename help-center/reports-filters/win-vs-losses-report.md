# Reports: Win vs Losses

The Win vs Losses report shows stats for all your winning and losing trades side-by-side, giving you a clear view of how your profitable trades compare to your losing ones.

## What This Report Shows

### Wins

- **Statistics (Wins):** Shows the statistics of all your winning trades, including key metrics like average win, win rate, and total profit.
- **Daily Net Cumulative P&L (Wins):** Displays how your total account P&L accumulated over the course of each trading day for winning trades.

### Losses

- **Statistics (Losses):** Shows the statistics of all your losing trades, including key metrics like average loss, loss rate, and total loss.
- **Daily Net Cumulative P&L (Losses):** Displays how your total account P&L accumulated over the course of each trading day for losing trades.

## How to Use This Report

Use the Win vs Losses report to compare your winning and losing trade behavior. Look for patterns such as whether your average win is larger than your average loss, how cumulative P&L trends differ between wins and losses, and whether specific time periods show different win/loss dynamics.
