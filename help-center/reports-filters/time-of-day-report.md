# Understanding TradeZella Time of Day Report

## Overview

The Time of Day Report segments your trading performance by hour, enabling you to identify the optimal times to trade and periods to avoid.

## Hourly Performance Analysis

## Performance by Hour

Examine your trading metrics across each hour of the trading day to spot patterns in your performance timing.

## Identifying Optimal Trading Windows

## Best Trading Hours

Discover the hours when you consistently achieve higher win rates and better profit factors, allowing you to concentrate your trading efforts during peak performance periods.

## Low-Performance Hours

Identify hours when your performance deteriorates, whether due to lower volatility, increased noise, or personal factors affecting decision-making quality.

## Key Metrics

- Net P&L per hour
- Trade count by hour
- Win rate by hour
- Profit factor by hour
