# Understanding TradeZella Reports Overview

## Overview

TradeZella Reports serves as a central hub for analyzing your trading performance with comprehensive metrics and visual representations of your trading activity.

## Key Metrics

The reports dashboard displays critical performance indicators:

- Net P&L - Your overall profit/loss
- Total Trades - Count of all executed trades
- Win Rate - Percentage of winning trades
- Average Win/Loss - Mean profit/loss per winning and losing trade
- Profit Factor - Ratio of gross profit to gross loss

## Visual Analysis

## Equity Curves

Graphical representation of your account growth over time, allowing you to visualize performance trends and drawdowns.

## Breakdowns by Dimension

Analyze performance across multiple segments including strategies, symbols, time periods, and custom tags to identify top performers.

## Filtering Options

Refine your analysis with powerful filtering capabilities:

- Date Range - Select specific time periods
- Account - Filter by trading account
- Strategy - Analyze individual strategy performance
- Tags - Custom categorization and grouping
