# Reports: Risk

TradeZella's Reports: Risk section helps you evaluate your trading through the lens of risk. By analyzing how trade volume, position sizing, and risk-reward multiples influence your outcomes, you can fine-tune your strategy to balance opportunity with protection. Access the Risk report from the Reports menu in your TradeZella dashboard.

## Available Tabs

- **Volume:** Review how different trade volumes impact your performance.
- **Position Size:** Assess the effect of position sizing on your outcomes.
- **R-Multiples:** Analyze trades based on R-Multiple categories (risk-reward ratio).

## What You'll See in Each Tab

### Performance Summary

A snapshot of your best and worst performing risk levels, most active risk levels (by volume or size), and highest win rate categories. Quickly identify where your risk-taking leads to the most consistent wins.

### Customizable Charts

Interactive charts to visualize your data. Plot up to three metrics side-by-side (e.g., Net P&L, Avg Drawdown, Trade Count) using bar or line charts. Available metrics include profitability (Net P&L, profit factor, trade expectancy), risk and drawdown (avg planned/realized R-Multiple, max drawdown), trading activity (trade count, volume), streaks and consistency (win %, consecutive wins/losses), and time analysis (avg hold time, trade duration).

### Summary Table

A detailed table listing stats for each volume range, position size, or R-Multiple level. Columns include profitability metrics, risk and drawdown, activity and volume breakdown, and streak data. Sort by any column to quickly identify your most or least successful risk levels.

### Cross Analysis

An advanced tool that applies a secondary filter to break down performance within each risk category. Cross-filter by account, playbook, tag, symbol, day and time, entry/exit time intervals, position size, or R-Multiple. This helps uncover hidden patterns — for example, which symbols handle larger position sizes best.

## Example Use Cases

- **Fine-tuning Position Size:** Identify which position size ranges consistently outperform and standardize around that level.
- **R-Multiple Focus:** Determine if high-R trades bring big wins or if lower-R trades provide steadier gains, and balance accordingly.
- **Volume vs. Symbol:** Discover which symbols handle larger volume trades better using Cross Analysis.
