# Zella Insights — AI-Powered Trading Analytics

## Overview

Zella Insights leverages artificial intelligence to automatically analyze your trading data, uncovering patterns and insights that manual review might miss.

## AI-Powered Analytics

## Automatic Trade Analysis

The system analyzes your entire trading history to identify recurring patterns, correlations, and performance anomalies without requiring manual investigation.

## Pattern Recognition

## Identifying Cross-Dimensional Patterns

Zella Insights identifies patterns that emerge across:

- Multiple strategies - How different strategies interact and perform relative to each other
- Time periods - Seasonal or temporal patterns in your trading behavior
- Symbols - Which instruments work best with your methods
- Account variations - How performance differs across trading accounts

## Data-Driven Recommendations

## Actionable Insights

The AI generates specific, actionable recommendations based on identified patterns and historical performance data.

## Performance Analysis

## Strengths Identification

Discover what you're doing right - the elements of your trading that consistently generate profitable results.

## Improvement Areas

Receive targeted suggestions on aspects of your trading that need refinement or adjustment based on empirical performance data.
