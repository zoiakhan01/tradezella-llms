# How to Update Your Payment Card Information

Keep your payment methods current to ensure uninterrupted service. Learn how to add, update, and manage payment methods in TradeZella.

## Why Update Payment Information

You may need to update your payment method when:

- Your credit card is expiring
- Your card is lost or replaced
- You want to use a different card
- Your billing address changes
- You need to update card security details
- Your current card is declined
- You want to add a backup payment method

## Accessing Payment Methods

### Via Settings

1. Log into your TradeZella account
2. Click your **avatar** or **username** in the top-right
3. Go to **Settings**
4. Select **"Billing"** or **"Payment Methods"**
5. View all saved payment methods

### Via Subscription Page

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Click **"Manage Billing"** or **"Payment Settings"**
3. View and edit payment methods

## Updating an Existing Card

### Edit Current Card

1. In **Billing** or **Payment Methods**
2. Find the card you want to update
3. Click **"Edit"** or the **three-dot menu**
4. Select **"Update Card"**

### Update Card Details

You can update:

- **Card Number**: If you received a replacement card
- **Expiration Date**: Check the new date on your card
- **CVV/CVC**: 3-digit security code on back of card
- **Cardholder Name**: If name changed
- **Billing Address**: Update if address changed
- **Zip Code**: Must match card's registered zip code

1. Edit the fields that have changed
2. Verify all information is correct
3. Click **"Save Changes"**
4. Confirmation appears

### Card Verification

After updating:
- Small charge ($0.01 or similar) may appear temporarily
- This verifies the card is valid
- Charge is reversed within 3-5 business days
- This is normal fraud prevention

## Adding a New Payment Method

To add an additional payment method:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing** > **Payment Methods**
2. Click **"Add Payment Method"** or **"+"** button
3. Select payment method type:
   - Credit Card
   - Debit Card
   - PayPal
   - Bank Account (ACH)
   - Digital Wallet

### Adding a Credit or Debit Card

1. Enter card number
2. Expiration date (MM/YY format)
3. CVV/CVC (3-4 digit security code)
4. Cardholder name
5. Billing address (must match card records)
6. Zip code
7. Click **"Add Card"**

Card is verified and added to your account.

### Adding PayPal

1. Click **"Connect PayPal"**
2. Redirected to PayPal login
3. Sign into your PayPal account
4. Authorize TradeZella to use PayPal
5. Returned to TradeZella
6. PayPal is added as payment option

### Adding Bank Account (ACH)

1. Enter bank name
2. Routing number (9 digits)
3. Account number
4. Account type (checking/savings)
5. Account holder name
6. Click **"Add Account"**

Bank account may require verification.

## Setting a Default Payment Method

Your default method is used for subscriptions and renewals:

1. Go to **Payment Methods**
2. Find the method you want as default
3. Click **"Set as Default"** or the radio button
4. Confirmation appears
5. New charges use this method

## Removing a Payment Method

To delete a payment method:

1. Go to **Payment Methods**
2. Find the method to remove
3. Click **"Delete"** or the **three-dot menu**
4. Select **"Remove"**
5. Confirm deletion

**Important**:
- Cannot delete your only payment method
- Add a new method before removing the old one
- If default, set a different default first

## Handling Declined Payments

If a charge is declined:

1. **Email Notification**: You receive an alert
2. **Update Payment**: Go to Payment Methods
3. **Verify Card**: Ensure card is valid and not expired
4. **Check Balance**: Ensure sufficient funds
5. **Contact Bank**: Ask if bank is blocking charges
6. **Try Again**: Attempt payment again with updated card

### Common Reasons for Declined Payments

- Card expired
- Insufficient funds
- Bank blocking transaction
- CVV incorrect
- Billing address mismatch
- Card flagged for fraud protection
- Payment limit reached

### How to Fix

- Update card expiration date
- Contact bank to verify address
- Add funds to account
- Use a different card
- Contact your bank's fraud department
- Update billing address to match bank records

## Updating Billing Address

Your billing address must match your bank's records:

1. Go to **Payment Methods**
2. Click the card to edit
3. Update the **Billing Address** field
4. Enter:
   - Street address
   - City
   - State/Province
   - Zip/Postal code
   - Country
5. Click **"Save"**

Address changes may prevent payment processing, so update it immediately if it changes.

## Security Best Practices

When updating payment information:

- Only update on secure, private internet connection
- Never share payment details via email or phone
- Use TradeZella website, not links from emails
- Never respond to payment requests via email
- Log out after updating payment information
- Check bank/credit statements regularly
- Report unauthorized charges immediately

## Multiple Cards for Different Purposes

You can add multiple cards:

- Personal credit card
- Business card
- Backup card
- Alternative payment method (PayPal, ACH)

Set your preferred card as default. You can change default anytime.

## Billing Cycle and Payment

Your selected payment method is charged:

- **Monthly Plans**: On your monthly billing date
- **Annual Plans**: On your annual billing date
- **Renewal Date**: Same day each cycle
- **Automatic Charge**: If auto-renewal is enabled

You can view upcoming charges in your Billing section.

## International Payment Methods

For international users:

- Most major credit cards accepted
- PayPal available worldwide
- Some regions support bank transfers
- Currency converted to USD
- See your country's payment options in Billing

## Payment Method Verification

When adding a new card:

- Small verification charge ($0.01-$1.00)
- Appears in 2-3 business days
- Automatically reversed
- Verifies card ownership
- Prevents fraud

## Viewing Payment History

To see all charges:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **"Invoices"** or **"Payment History"**
3. View all charges and dates
4. Download invoices as PDF
5. See payment method used for each charge

## Troubleshooting Payment Issues

**Q: My payment is declined but I have funds**
A: Contact your bank - they may be blocking the charge. Ask them to authorize TradeZella charges.

**Q: I don't see the verification charge**
A: It may take 2-3 business days to appear. Check later or contact your bank.

**Q: Can I pay by invoice?**
A: Contact TradeZella Support for enterprise billing options.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes, all payments use PCI-DSS compliant encryption. Your data is never stored unencrypted.
