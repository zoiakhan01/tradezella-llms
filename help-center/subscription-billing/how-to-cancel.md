# How to Cancel Your Subscription

You can cancel your TradeZella subscription at any time. This guide explains how to cancel, what happens to your data, and your options after cancellation.

## Before You Cancel

Before cancelling, consider:

- **Data Access**: Your trade data remains accessible after cancellation
- **Premium Features**: You'll lose access to advanced features
- **Limitations**: Free tier has restricted functionality
- **Re-subscription**: You can resubscribe anytime with your data intact
- **No Refunds**: Cancellation typically doesn't include refunds for paid period

## How to Cancel Your Subscription

### Step 1: Access Subscription Settings

1. Log into your TradeZella account
2. Click your **avatar** or **username** in the top-right corner
3. Select **"Subscription"** or go to **Settings**
4. Navigate to **"Billing"** or **"Subscription Management"**

### Step 2: Find Cancellation Option

1. In your subscription section, look for:
   - **"Cancel Subscription"** button
   - **"Manage Subscription"** > **"Cancel"**
   - Three-dot menu next to your subscription
2. Click the cancellation option

### Step 3: Reason for Cancellation (Optional)

You may be asked why you're cancelling:

- Select a reason from the list:
  - Too expensive
  - Not using features
  - Found alternative
  - Temporary break
  - Other reasons
- This helps TradeZella understand user needs
- Providing feedback is optional

### Step 4: Confirm Cancellation

1. Review the cancellation details:
   - Current expiration date
   - When subscription ends
   - What happens to your data
   - Free tier details
2. Review any special offers (discounts to stay)
3. Click **"Confirm Cancellation"** to finalize

### Step 5: Confirmation

1. Cancellation is confirmed immediately
2. Confirmation email sent to your address
3. Your subscription will end on your current expiration date
4. You'll have access until that date

## What Happens After Cancellation

### During the Current Period

Until your subscription expires:
- Full access to all premium features
- All functionality works normally
- Can still track trades, view reports, etc.

### After Expiration

Once your subscription ends:

- **Downgrade to Free Tier**: Your account becomes a free tier account
- **Data Preserved**: All your trades and accounts remain
- **Limited Features**: Some advanced features become unavailable
- **View-Only Access**: Can view trades and reports
- **No New Tracking**: Cannot add or import new trades
- **Basic Features**: Calendar and basic charts still available

### Free Tier Limitations

After cancellation, you:

- Can view your trade history
- Can view performance reports
- Cannot add new trades
- Cannot import new data
- Cannot edit trade details
- Have limited export options
- Cannot use advanced filtering
- No access to advanced analytics

## Disabling Auto-Renewal Instead

Instead of cancelling, you can disable auto-renewal:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Find **"Auto-Renewal"** setting
3. Toggle **OFF**
4. Save changes

Benefits:
- Your subscription continues normally
- No auto-renewal after expiration
- You decide later whether to renew
- No data loss
- Easier to resume later

This keeps your account active without committing to renewal.

## Cancelled Subscription Options

### Resubscribe Anytime

You can resubscribe at any time:

1. As a free tier user, click **"Upgrade"** or **"Subscribe"**
2. Choose your plan
3. Complete payment
4. Immediately regain full access
5. All your data is still there

### Data Backup Before Cancelling

Before cancelling, you may want to backup your data:

1. Go to your account
2. Look for **"Export"** or **"Download Data"** option
3. Export trades as CSV or PDF
4. Download performance reports
5. Save files to your computer

This preserves your records independently.

### Contacting Support

If cancellation is permanent, contact Support:

1. Have account information ready
2. Request data export if needed
3. Ask about any special circumstances
4. Support can provide options

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I get a refund when I cancel?**
A: TradeZella typically doesn't offer refunds for paid periods. You have access until your current period ends.

**Q: What if I paid for a year but only used a month?**
A: Cancellation still doesn't provide refunds. However, you have full access for the entire year.

**Q: Can I cancel mid-month or mid-year?**
A: Yes, you can cancel anytime. Your subscription ends on the original expiration date.

**Q: Will my data be deleted?**
A: No, your data remains even after cancellation. You can view it as a free tier user.

**Q: Can I cancel and resubscribe to the same plan later?**
A: Absolutely. You can cancel and resubscribe anytime. Your data persists.

**Q: What if I have auto-renewal on?**
A: Disable auto-renewal before cancelling if you don't want automatic charges.

**Q: Will my payment method still be charged?**
A: No, once cancelled (with auto-renewal off), no further charges occur.

**Q: How long does cancellation take?**
A: It's immediate. Your subscription status updates right away.

## After Cancellation Checklist

- Confirm cancellation email received
- Verify access remains until expiration date
- Export important data if desired
- Update payment methods if resubscribing
- Note free tier limitations
- Plan if/when to resubscribe

## Alternative: Pause Subscription

If you need a temporary break:

- Contact TradeZella Support
- Request subscription pause
- Account frozen for specified period
- Resumes without resubscribing
- May not be available in all regions
