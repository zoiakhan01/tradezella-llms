# How to Renew Your Subscription

Renewing your TradeZella subscription ensures uninterrupted access to all premium features and trading tools. You can renew your subscription manually before expiration or set up automatic renewal to avoid service interruption.

## Subscription Expiration Timeline

Understanding your subscription status:

- **Active Subscription**: Your subscription is current and active
- **Expiration Date**: Shows when your current subscription ends
- **Grace Period**: 7 days after expiration with limited features
- **Suspended Account**: After grace period, account is suspended
- **Data Access**: You can still access your data, but cannot trade/track new trades

You'll receive email reminders before expiration:
- 30 days before expiration
- 7 days before expiration
- On expiration date

## Manual Renewal

### Method 1: Via Your Account

1. Log into your TradeZella account
2. Click your **avatar** or **username** in the top-right
3. Select **"Subscription"** or **"Billing"**
4. Look for your subscription status
5. Click **"Renew Now"** or **"Renew Subscription"**
6. Review your plan details
7. Confirm your payment method
8. Click **"Renew"** to process
9. Receipt will be sent to your email

### Method 2: Via Billing Settings

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Find your active subscription
3. Click **"Manage Subscription"**
4. Select **"Renew Now"**
5. Follow the payment steps
6. Confirmation appears on screen

### Method 3: Email Link

1. When you receive the expiration reminder email
2. Click **"Renew Your Subscription"** link
3. You'll be taken to the renewal page (if logged in)
4. Complete the renewal process
5. Instant confirmation

## Automatic Renewal

Setting up auto-renewal ensures you never lose access:

### Enabling Auto-Renewal

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing** > **Subscription**
2. Find **"Auto-Renewal"** option
3. Toggle **"Enable Automatic Renewal"** to ON
4. Confirm your current payment method
5. Click **"Save"**

With auto-renewal enabled:
- Your subscription automatically renews on the expiration date
- Your payment method is charged automatically
- You receive a renewal confirmation email
- No action needed on your part

### Disabling Auto-Renewal

To turn off automatic renewal:

1. Go to **Billing** > **Subscription**
2. Find **"Auto-Renewal"** setting
3. Toggle OFF to disable
4. Confirm the action
5. Click **"Save"**

Once disabled:
- Your subscription will expire on the set date
- You'll need to manually renew to continue
- Auto-renewal can be re-enabled anytime

## Payment Methods

TradeZella accepts multiple payment methods for renewal:

- **Credit Card**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- **PayPal**: If you have a PayPal account on file
- **Bank Transfer**: In some regions (ACH)
- **Digital Wallets**: Apple Pay, Google Pay

Your saved payment method is used for renewal. You can update it before renewing.

## Updating Payment Information

Before renewal, update your payment method:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **"Payment Methods"** or **"Edit Payment"**
3. Update card information:
   - Card number
   - Expiration date
   - CVV
   - Billing address
4. Click **"Save"**
5. Set as default if needed

## Renewal Pricing

### Same Price as Current Plan

Renewal is typically at the same price as your current plan:
- Annual plans renew at annual price
- Monthly plans renew at monthly price
- Promotional pricing carries over if still valid

### Price Changes

If TradeZella changes pricing:
- You'll be notified 30 days before renewal
- Renewal may be at a new price
- You can cancel before renewal takes effect
- Or choose a different plan

### Discount Codes

You may apply discount codes during renewal:

1. On the renewal confirmation page
2. Enter your discount code
3. Code is validated and applied
4. Total price reflects the discount
5. Complete payment

## Renewal Confirmation

After successful renewal:

1. **Instant Confirmation**: Appears on screen immediately
2. **Email Receipt**: Sent to your registered email
3. **Invoice**: Can be downloaded from Billing section
4. **Subscription Extended**: Expiration date moves forward
5. **Full Access**: All premium features remain active

## Troubleshooting Renewal Issues

### Payment Declined

If your payment is declined:

1. Check your card isn't expired
2. Ensure sufficient funds
3. Contact your card issuer to allow TradeZella charges
4. Try a different payment method
5. Use alternative payment option (PayPal, etc.)

### Renewal Failed

If renewal failed:

1. Check the error message
2. Verify payment information is correct
3. Try renewing again
4. Contact TradeZella Support for assistance
5. Can also renew via different method

### Missing Renewal Email

If you don't receive reminders:

1. Check spam/promotions folder
2. Verify email address in Settings
3. Update email address if needed
4. Re-enable email notifications
5. Set calendar reminder yourself

## Renewal FAQs

**Q: When does my renewal take effect?**
A: Immediately upon successful payment. Your expiration date extends by one subscription period.

**Q: Can I change my plan at renewal?**
A: Yes, you can switch to a different plan during renewal. See "How to Switch Your Plan" article.

**Q: Can I renew early?**
A: Generally no, but contact Support for special circumstances. Time added typically starts from your current expiration date.

**Q: What if I renew but then cancel?**
A: You'll have access through the renewal period. Cancellation takes effect at next renewal date.
