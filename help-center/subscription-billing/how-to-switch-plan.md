# How to Switch Your TradeZella Subscription Plan

Easily upgrade, downgrade, or change your TradeZella subscription plan at any time. Plan changes take effect immediately, and we'll handle any prorated charges or credits.

## Available TradeZella Plans

TradeZella offers several subscription tiers:

### Starter Plan
- Basic trade tracking
- Up to 3 accounts
- Manual trade entry only
- Basic reporting
- Monthly billing

### Pro Plan
- Unlimited accounts
- Broker integration and auto-sync
- Advanced reporting and analytics
- Performance metrics
- Monthly or annual billing

### Premium Plan
- All Pro features
- Advanced portfolio analysis
- Custom reports and exports
- Priority support
- Monthly or annual billing

### Trading Team Plan
- Multiple user access
- Team collaboration features
- Advanced permissions
- Centralized billing
- Annual billing

Each plan has different features and price points. Review your current plan's limitations before switching.

## How to Switch Plans

### Step 1: Access Your Subscription

1. Log into your TradeZella account
2. Click your **avatar** or **username** in the top-right
3. Select **"Subscription"** or go to **Settings**
4. Navigate to **"Billing"** > **"Subscription Plans"**

### Step 2: View Available Plans

1. You'll see all available plans with features compared
2. Your current plan is highlighted
3. See pricing for monthly and annual billing
4. Compare features between plans
5. Click on a different plan to select it

### Step 3: Choose Your Plan

1. Click **"Switch to [Plan Name]"** or **"Upgrade/Downgrade"**
2. Confirm the plan details
3. Review what changes (new features or limitations)
4. See proration calculation if applicable
5. Click **"Continue"** to proceed

### Step 4: Billing Adjustment

1. TradeZella calculates proration:
   - Amount owed if upgrading (credit toward new plan)
   - Amount refunded if downgrading
   - Based on remaining days in current period
2. Review the new billing amount
3. Confirm your payment method
4. See new expiration date

### Step 5: Confirm Switch

1. Review all changes one final time
2. New plan details
3. Pricing and billing date
4. Features you'll gain or lose
5. Click **"Confirm Plan Change"**
6. Instant confirmation on screen

## Plan Change Scenarios

### Upgrading Plans

When you upgrade to a higher-tier plan:

- **Immediate Access**: New features available right away
- **Proration**: Credit applied if upgrading mid-period
  - Example: Upgrade from Starter to Pro on day 15 of 30
  - Starter cost: $9.99/month
  - Pro cost: $24.99/month
  - Credit for remaining 15 days of Starter
  - Pay difference for Pro
- **No Data Loss**: All accounts and trades remain
- **Billing Date**: New expiration date based on plan length

### Downgrading Plans

When you downgrade to a lower-tier plan:

- **Feature Limitations**: Some features become unavailable
  - Excess accounts may need to be archived
  - Advanced features disabled
- **Refund Calculation**: You may receive a credit for unused time
- **Effective Date**: Change takes effect immediately or on next billing
- **Data Preserved**: Your historical data remains accessible

Example downgrade:
- Pro ($24.99/month), day 20 of 30
- Downgrade to Starter ($9.99/month)
- Receive credit for 10 remaining days
- Credit applied to next billing or refunded

### Switching Plan Types

Monthly to Annual (or vice versa):

- **Annual Plans**: Better pricing (usually 15-20% discount)
- **Monthly Plans**: Pay as you go, no long-term commitment
- **Switching**: Proration applies
- **Timing**: Choose when to make the switch

## Proration Explained

Proration ensures you only pay for the time you use:

### Upgrade Example

- Current plan: Starter ($9.99/month) - 15 days used
- Upgrade to: Pro ($24.99/month)
- Days remaining: 15 days
- Starter daily rate: $0.33/day × 15 = $4.95 credit
- Pro daily rate: $0.83/day × 15 = $12.45 due
- Total: Pay $7.50 (difference)
- New expiration: 30 days from today (new cycle)

### Downgrade Example

- Current plan: Premium ($44.99/month) - 10 days used
- Downgrade to: Pro ($24.99/month)
- Days remaining: 20 days
- Premium daily rate: $1.50/day × 20 = $30 credit
- Pro daily rate: $0.83/day × 20 = $16.60 due
- Total: Receive $13.40 credit

Credit typically:
- Applied to next billing cycle
- Or refunded to original payment method

## Managing Multiple Plans

If you have multiple accounts or use TradeZella with a team:

- Each account can have its own plan
- User accounts may share a team plan
- Billing is consolidated to main account
- View all associated subscriptions in one place

## Plan Switching Restrictions

Some limitations apply:

- **Downgrade Limits**: Cannot downgrade if using excluded features
  - Example: Must archive excess accounts before downgrading
  - Must remove team users before downgrading to single-user plan
- **Cooling Period**: Some plans have minimum periods (rarely)
- **Free Trial**: Switching from free trial starts billing immediately

## After Switching Plans

### Immediate Changes

- New plan features activate immediately
- Old features remain until plan change fully processes
- Billing amount updates for next cycle
- Email confirmation sent

### What to Expect

1. **Confirmation Email**: Details of plan change
2. **Updated Invoice**: Shows new billing amount
3. **Feature Access**: New features available in account
4. **Expiration Date**: Extended or adjusted based on plan

### Accessing New Features

After switching up:
1. Log out and back in (may be needed)
2. New features appear in menu
3. May need to configure new features
4. Access advanced settings and reports

## Switching Back

You can switch plans repeatedly:

- No limits on switching
- Each switch is handled independently
- Previous plan is no longer available if downgraded
- Can always upgrade again later

## Plan Switch Pricing

You're charged:
- Only for the difference in plan costs (if upgrading)
- Or receive a credit (if downgrading)
- Calculated daily for exact proration
- No cancellation fees

## Payment Method for Switch

- Uses your current saved payment method
- Or choose a different payment method during switch
- Update your payment method in Billing settings
- Ensure payment method has sufficient funds

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does the plan change take?**
A: Instantly. Changes appear on your account right away.

**Q: Do I lose my data when switching plans?**
A: No, all your data remains intact regardless of plan changes.

**Q: Can I switch mid-billing cycle?**
A: Yes, and proration is applied automatically.

**Q: What if I have auto-renewal?**
A: Your new plan auto-renews at the new price.

**Q: Can I switch to annual billing to save money?**
A: Yes, switching to annual plans typically offers a discount.
