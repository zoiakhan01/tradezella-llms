# TradeZella Billing - How Upgrades, Proration, and Renewals Work

Understanding TradeZella's billing system helps you manage your subscription costs effectively. Learn how proration works, how upgrades and downgrades are billed, and how renewals are calculated.

## Billing Periods

TradeZella offers two billing period options:

### Monthly Billing
- Charged every 30 days
- Subscription renews on the same date each month
- More flexibility; easier to cancel anytime
- Typically higher monthly cost
- Best for: Testing services, varying needs

### Annual Billing
- Charged once per year
- Better value; typically 15-20% discount
- Longer commitment
- Locked-in price for the year
- Best for: Long-term users, cost savings

## Billing Dates and Renewal

Your billing date is when your subscription renews and you're charged:

### First Billing
- Occurs when you purchase your subscription
- Amount charged: Full price of your plan
- Date set: One period from purchase date

### Renewal Billing
- Occurs every month or year based on your plan
- Amount charged: Full price of your plan
- Same date each cycle
- Can be automated or manual

### Viewing Your Billing Date
1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Find your subscription details
3. See "Next Billing Date" or "Renewal Date"
4. Plan length shows under your plan name

## Proration Explained

Proration is how TradeZella ensures you pay only for the time you use. When you upgrade, downgrade, or switch plans mid-period, proration calculates the difference.

### How Proration Works

**Basic Principle**: Your cost per day for each plan level is calculated, then applied for the days used.

**Formula**:
- Plan cost ÷ Days in billing period = Daily rate
- Daily rate × Days remaining = Amount owed or credited

### Upgrade Example

Scenario: You've had a $9.99/month Starter plan for 10 days and want to upgrade to Pro ($24.99/month).

**Calculation**:
- Days in period: 30 days
- Days used: 10 days
- Days remaining: 20 days
- Starter daily rate: $9.99 ÷ 30 = $0.333/day
- Starter already paid: $0.333 × 10 = $3.33 (sunk cost)
- Starter remaining credit: $0.333 × 20 = $6.67
- Pro daily rate: $24.99 ÷ 30 = $0.833/day
- Pro charges for 20 days: $0.833 × 20 = $16.66
- Net charge: $16.66 - $6.67 = **$9.99** (paid $9.99, receive new plan)

**Result**: You pay $9.99 to upgrade. Your new expiration date is 20 days from now.

### Downgrade Example

Scenario: You've had a $24.99/month Pro plan for 15 days and want to downgrade to Starter ($9.99/month).

**Calculation**:
- Days in period: 30 days
- Days used: 15 days
- Days remaining: 15 days
- Pro daily rate: $24.99 ÷ 30 = $0.833/day
- Pro already paid: $0.833 × 15 = $12.49 (sunk cost)
- Pro remaining credit: $0.833 × 15 = $12.49
- Starter daily rate: $9.99 ÷ 30 = $0.333/day
- Starter charges for 15 days: $0.333 × 15 = $5.00
- Net credit: $12.49 - $5.00 = **$7.49** (receive credit)

**Result**: You receive a $7.49 credit. Your new expiration date is 15 days from now.

## Monthly to Annual Switch

When switching from monthly to annual billing mid-period:

**Example**:
- Current: Starter monthly ($9.99/month) - 20 days into 30-day cycle
- Switch to: Starter annual ($99.90/year or $8.33/month equivalent)
- Days remaining: 10 days

**Calculation**:
- Monthly credit remaining: $9.99 ÷ 30 × 10 = $3.33
- Annual daily rate: $99.90 ÷ 365 = $0.274/day
- Annual charges for 10 days: $0.274 × 10 = $2.74
- Net charge: $3.33 - $2.74 = **$0.59** (receive credit)

**Result**: Pay $0.59 to switch to annual. New expiration: 365 days from today.

## Plan Change Expiration Dates

When you change plans, your expiration date adjusts:

### Upgrade
- Remaining days from old plan carry over
- New plan cycle begins
- Expiration: Original expiration date + adjustment

### Downgrade
- Remaining days apply to new plan
- New expiration: Same number of days from now
- Example: 20 days remaining → 20 days remaining (just different plan)

### Monthly to Annual
- New expiration: Exactly 365 days from today
- Previous expiration ignored
- Full year of service

## Annual Discount

Annual billing typically provides 15-20% savings:

**Example**:
- Monthly: $9.99/month × 12 = $119.88/year
- Annual: $99.90/year
- Savings: $19.98 (16.7% off)

The annual billing price is locked in. If prices increase next year, you maintain the current rate for your renewal year.

## Renewals and Auto-Renewal

### How Renewals Work

1. Renewal date approaches (you get email reminders)
2. If auto-renewal enabled, payment is processed automatically
3. If manual, you manually renew on your account
4. New billing period begins
5. Expiration date extends by another period

### Auto-Renewal

Auto-renewal automatically charges your payment method:

**Enabling Auto-Renewal**:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Find **"Auto-Renewal"** option
3. Toggle ON
4. Confirm payment method
5. Saves changes

**Disabling Auto-Renewal**:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Toggle **"Auto-Renewal"** OFF
3. Subscription expires on current date
4. You can manually renew later
5. No charges will occur without action

### Renewal Pricing

You typically renew at your current plan price:
- Same price as your existing plan
- Price may have changed since purchase (you maintain old price)
- Promotional pricing carries over if still valid
- You can use discount codes at renewal

## Subscription Cancellation and Credits

### Cancellation Timing

You can cancel anytime. Cancellation takes effect:

- **Immediate**: Auto-renewal is disabled immediately
- **At Expiration**: You have access until current period ends
- **No Refund**: Generally, no refund for unused time
- **Data Preserved**: Your accounts and trades remain

### Pro-Rated Cancellation

Some scenarios offer prorated refunds:

- **Enterprise Plans**: May offer refunds on request
- **Billing Errors**: Credits issued for duplicate charges
- **Service Issues**: May receive credits or refunds
- Contact Support for special circumstances

## International Billing

For international users:

### Currency Conversion
- Prices may be converted to local currency
- Conversion happens at payment time
- Exchange rates apply
- Receipt shows USD amount

### Payment Methods
- Most major credit cards accepted globally
- PayPal available in most countries
- Bank transfers (ACH) available in some regions
- Check your region's options in Billing

### VAT/GST
- Some regions charge additional tax
- EU customers: VAT may apply
- You may need to provide tax ID
- Price shown may be before tax

## Taxes and Invoices

### Invoices

View invoices for tax records:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **"Invoices"** or **"Payment History"**
3. See all charges and dates
4. Download as PDF
5. Save for accounting records

### Tax Information

- TradeZella provides invoices showing charges
- Consult accountant about business deductions
- May be business expense depending on jurisdiction
- Keep records for tax purposes

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if I upgrade/downgrade on the last day of my billing period?**
A: Proration still applies. The remaining days of service are credited/charged fairly.

**Q: Do I get refunded if I cancel?**
A: Not typically. You have access until your current period ends. Contact Support for exceptions.

**Q: Can I use a discount code during renewal?**
A: Yes, you can apply a discount code at the renewal confirmation step.

**Q: What happens if my payment is declined at renewal?**
A: You'll receive email reminders. You can retry payment, update your card, or contact Support.

**Q: Can I change my billing date?**
A: Not directly, but contact Support for special requests.

**Q: Is my billing information secure?**
A: Yes, all payments are PCI-DSS compliant with 256-bit encryption.

**Q: Do you offer invoicing instead of recurring charges?**
A: Contact Sales for enterprise billing options and invoicing.

**Q: What currencies do you accept?**
A: Primarily USD, but accepted cards handle currency conversion.
