# Zella Scale — Maximize Your Trade Insights

Zella Scale displays the gap between your potential profit/loss and your actual profit/loss, giving you powerful insights for self-improvement.

## Understand Your Trade Potential

Zella Scale shows you what you could have made (based on MFE) compared to what you actually made. This gap reveals opportunities you may have missed or exits that were too early or too late.

## View Across Multiple Pages

Access Zella Scale on:

- Trade Log
- Daily Journal
- Trade Tracking pages

## Improve Your Trading

By regularly reviewing your Zella Scale metrics, you can identify patterns in your trading execution. Are you consistently taking profits too early? Exiting too late? This tool helps you recognize these patterns and adjust your strategy accordingly.
