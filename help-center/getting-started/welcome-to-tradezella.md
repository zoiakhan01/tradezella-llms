# Welcome to TradeZella

TradeZella provides comprehensive tools to manage and analyze your trading activity. Here's what you can do with our platform:

## Key Features

**Dashboard** - Get performance snapshots and key metrics at a glance.

**Trade Log** - Manage all your trades in one centralized location.

**Daily Journal** - Break down your trading activity day-by-day with detailed notes.

**Reports** - Access 50+ analytics to track performance and identify patterns.

**Strategies** - Create and track your trading playbooks and rule-based systems.

**Notebook** - Keep a personal journal for your trading thoughts and reflections.

**Backtesting** - Validate your strategies before deploying them with real capital.

**Trade Replay** - Review and study your past trades to improve future decisions.

**Spaces/Mentor Mode** - Share your progress and get coaching from experienced traders.

**Prop Firm Sync** - Track your performance on trading challenges and prop firm accounts.

**Zella University** - Access educational content to develop your trading skills.

**Community** - Connect with other traders on our Discord community.

Start by setting up your accounts and importing your trades to begin leveraging these features.
