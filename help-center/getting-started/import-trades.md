# Get Your Trades Imported into TradeZella

Get your trading history into TradeZella using one of three flexible import methods.

## Import Methods

### 1. Auto-Sync (Recommended)

Connect your broker account directly to TradeZella for automatic trade import:
- Trades are synced in real-time or on a scheduled basis
- No manual work needed once connected
- Supported brokers include: MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, cTrader, and Tradovate

Follow the step-by-step instructions in the import wizard to connect your broker.

### 2. File Upload

Export your trades as CSV or Excel from your broker and upload to TradeZella:
- Supports 500+ brokers and prop firms
- Works with any file format your broker exports
- Use the import wizard to map fields and validate your data

This is ideal if your broker doesn't support direct auto-sync but can export trade data.

### 3. Manual Entry

Enter your trades by hand with full execution details:
- Perfect for paper trading or trades executed outside standard platforms
- Works for any broker or trading method
- Enter price, quantity, entry time, exit time, and other details

You also have access to a generic CSV format for brokers that aren't yet specifically integrated. Check the import wizard for format specifications.

## Getting Started

Navigate to the import section and choose the method that works best for your situation. The import wizard will guide you through each step with clear instructions.
