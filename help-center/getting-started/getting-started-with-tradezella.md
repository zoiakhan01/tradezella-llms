# Getting Started with TradeZella

Follow these six steps to get up and running with TradeZella:

## Step 1: Layout Overview

Familiarize yourself with the platform interface:
- **Sidebar Navigation** - Access all major features and settings
- **Dashboard** - Your central hub for performance metrics and trade overview
- **Settings** - Customize your account preferences and integrations

## Step 2: Import Trades

Get your historical trades into the system using one of three methods:
- **Auto-Sync** - Connect your broker account directly for automatic import
- **File Upload** - Export from your broker and upload CSV/Excel files
- **Manual Entry** - Enter trades manually with full execution details

## Step 3: Navigate Your Dashboard

Make the most of your trading overview:
- Customize dashboard widgets to display your key metrics
- Use pre-built templates or create your own dashboard layout

## Step 4: Create Tags

Organize your trades for better analysis:
- Create tags like "momentum", "breakout", "FOMC", or any custom categories
- Use tags to categorize and filter trades by strategy or market conditions

## Step 5: Set Up Strategies

Build your trading framework:
- Create trading rules and playbooks
- Use strategy templates for common approaches
- Track performance by strategy

## Step 6: Daily Workflow

Establish a routine to maximize your trading insights:
- Review your completed trades each day
- Write journal notes reflecting on your decisions
- Check Zella Insights for AI-powered analysis and recommendations

You're now ready to leverage TradeZella's full suite of analytics and tracking tools!
