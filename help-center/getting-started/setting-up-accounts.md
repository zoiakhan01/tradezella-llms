# Setting Up Accounts within TradeZella

Manage multiple trading accounts and control how they're tracked in TradeZella.

## Account Management Actions

### Add New Accounts
Create new accounts from the Add Trade page. Simply click to create a new account and give it a name.

### Delete Accounts
Remove accounts you no longer need. Note: This action is irreversible, so ensure you've backed up any important data.

### Adjust Account Balance
Manually update your account balance at any time to reflect deposits, withdrawals, or corrections.

### Archive Accounts
Archive old or inactive accounts. Archived accounts:
- Don't count toward your account limits
- Cannot be unarchived once archived
- Remain in your account history for reference

### Transfer Trades
Move trades between accounts with ease. Use this if you need to reorganize trades across multiple accounts.

### Clear All Trades
Remove all trades from an account without deleting the account itself. Useful for resetting an account while keeping its settings.

## Account Limits by Plan

- **Basic Plan** - 1 account
- **Pro Plan** - Unlimited accounts

Upgrade to the Pro Plan if you need to manage multiple trading accounts across different brokers or strategies.
