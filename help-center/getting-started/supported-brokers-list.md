# List of Supported Brokers and Platforms

TradeZella supports a wide range of brokers and trading platforms through multiple integration methods.

## Auto-Sync Brokers

Connect your account directly for automatic trade synchronization:
- MetaTrader 4
- MetaTrader 5
- cTrader
- Tradovate

## File Upload Brokers (500+)

Export your trades and upload them to TradeZella. Supported platforms include:
- ThinkorSwim
- NinjaTrader
- Interactive Brokers
- Webull
- TradeStation
- Tastyworks
- Charles Schwab
- E*TRADE
- Fidelity
- Robinhood
- TD Ameritrade
- Tradovate
- Sierra Chart
- Quantower
- MotiveWave
- DXTRADE
- Match-Trader
- TradeLocker
- And 500+ more

## Manual Entry

Enter trades manually for any broker not listed above. This works with any trading platform or method.

## Generic CSV Format

TradeZella also supports a generic CSV format for brokers without specific integrations. Check the import wizard for format specifications.

## New Brokers Coming Soon

We're constantly adding new broker integrations based on user requests. If your broker isn't listed, let us know and we'll prioritize adding support for it. You can always use file upload or manual entry in the meantime.
