# About Zella AI

Zella AI is TradeZella's intelligent assistant designed to help you analyze and improve your trading performance.

## Current Features

### Zella Insights

Our AI-powered analytics engine automatically analyzes your trade data to:
- Surface patterns in your trading behavior
- Identify anomalies and unusual trades
- Provide actionable recommendations for improvement

Access Zella Insights in the Reports section to see AI-generated analysis of your trading performance.

## Coming Soon

We're actively developing additional AI capabilities:

### Full AI Chatbot

Ask questions about your trading performance and get instant, personalized answers. Query your trade history, ask for analysis, and get insights in natural language.

### AI-Generated Trade Reviews

Receive detailed reviews of individual trades with analysis of your entry, exit, risk management, and decision-making process.

### Personalized Improvement Plans

Get custom trading improvement plans based on your unique trading data, patterns, and weaknesses. These plans will be tailored to your trading style and goals.
