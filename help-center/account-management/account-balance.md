# Setting Your Account Balance in TradeZella

Your account balance in TradeZella represents the total capital in your trading account. Properly setting and maintaining your balance is essential for accurate P&L calculations, ROI measurements, and portfolio performance tracking.

## Initial Balance Setup

When you create a new account, you'll need to set the initial balance:

1. During account creation, enter your starting capital in the **"Initial Balance"** field
2. This should reflect your actual account balance when you begin tracking trades
3. The initial balance serves as the baseline for all P&L calculations
4. You cannot modify the initial balance after account creation without archiving and recreating

## Recording Account Balance Changes

### Adding Deposits

When you add money to your trading account:

1. Go to your account's **"Balance"** or **"Account Settings"** section
2. Click **"Record Deposit"** or **"Add Funds"**
3. Enter the deposit amount
4. Enter the date of the deposit
5. Click **"Save"**

The new balance is automatically calculated and all future trades reflect the updated capital.

### Recording Withdrawals

When you withdraw funds from your trading account:

1. Navigate to your account settings
2. Click **"Record Withdrawal"** or **"Remove Funds"**
3. Enter the withdrawal amount
4. Enter the date of withdrawal
5. Click **"Save"**

Your balance will be adjusted immediately.

## Balance Adjustments

Sometimes you may need to adjust your balance for:

- **Interest or Dividends**: Add the amount received
- **Fees or Charges**: Subtract broker fees or account maintenance charges
- **Corrections**: Fix balance discrepancies with your broker

### Making Balance Adjustments

1. Access your account's **"Balance History"** or **"Adjustments"** section
2. Click **"Add Adjustment"**
3. Select the adjustment type (deposit, withdrawal, or manual adjustment)
4. Enter the amount and date
5. Optionally add a note explaining the adjustment
6. Click **"Save"**

## Viewing Balance History

Your balance history shows all deposits, withdrawals, and adjustments:

1. Go to **"Account Settings"** > **"Balance History"**
2. View a chronological list of all balance changes
3. See the date, amount, and type of each transaction
4. Edit or delete recent entries if corrections are needed

## Impact on P&L Calculations

Your account balance affects:

- **Return on Investment (ROI)**: Calculated as profit divided by account balance
- **Risk Metrics**: Percentage-based risk calculations use current balance
- **Position Size Analysis**: Percentage of account per trade
- **Account Statistics**: Year-to-date and lifetime performance metrics

For accurate performance tracking, ensure your balance always reflects reality.

## Synced Broker Accounts

If your account is synced with your broker (Schwab, Interactive Brokers, etc.):

- Balance may auto-update based on broker data
- You may not need to manually record deposits/withdrawals
- Check your broker sync settings in Account Settings
- Sync status appears next to your account balance

## Manual vs. Synced Balances

- **Manual Accounts**: You record all deposits and withdrawals yourself
- **Synced Accounts**: TradeZella pulls balance data from your broker

Verify which type your account is to know whether manual updates are needed.
