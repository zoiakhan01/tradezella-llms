# How to Change Your Timezone in TradeZella

Your timezone setting in TradeZella determines how all timestamps, market hours, and trade times are displayed. Setting the correct timezone ensures your trading data aligns with your actual trading schedule and local time.

## Why Timezone Matters

Your timezone affects:

- **Trade Entry/Exit Times**: When trades are recorded and displayed
- **Market Hours**: Market open/close times for your assets
- **Performance Reports**: Daily and intraday P&L calculations
- **Import Data**: Imported trades are converted to your timezone
- **Notifications**: Alert timing matches your timezone
- **Chart Display**: Candle times and price action timing

## Changing Your Timezone

### Method 1: Via Profile Settings

1. Click your **avatar** or **username** in the top-right corner
2. Select **"Profile Settings"**
3. Look for the **"Timezone"** dropdown
4. Click the timezone dropdown to see available options
5. Search for your timezone by:
   - City name (e.g., "New York", "London")
   - Region (e.g., "Eastern", "Pacific")
   - UTC offset (e.g., "UTC-5")
6. Select your timezone
7. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Method 2: Via Account Settings

1. Go to **"Accounts"** section
2. Select your trading account
3. Click **"Settings"**
4. Find **"Timezone"** in the account settings
5. Select your desired timezone
6. Click **"Save"**

Note: You can set different timezones for different accounts if needed.

## Timezone Format Reference

TradeZella displays timezones in standard format:

- **UTC Offset**: Shows your offset from Coordinated Universal Time
- **Daylight Saving**: Automatically adjusts for DST when applicable
- **City Reference**: Often shows major city in your timezone

**Examples**:
- Eastern Time (ET): UTC-5 (winter) or UTC-4 (summer)
- Central Time (CT): UTC-6 (winter) or UTC-5 (summer)
- Mountain Time (MT): UTC-7 (winter) or UTC-6 (summer)
- Pacific Time (PT): UTC-8 (winter) or UTC-7 (summer)
- London: UTC+0 (winter) or UTC+1 (summer)
- Tokyo: UTC+9 (no DST)

## Daylight Saving Time

TradeZella automatically handles daylight saving transitions:

- **Spring Forward**: Clocks move forward when DST starts
- **Fall Back**: Clocks move back when DST ends
- **Automatic Adjustment**: Times automatically adjust in TradeZella
- **No Manual Action Needed**: You don't need to manually change settings

However, manually verify after DST transitions to ensure correct times.

## Setting Timezone for Each Account

You can configure separate timezones for different accounts:

1. Each account has its own timezone setting
2. Useful if you trade multiple markets in different zones
3. Set each account individually in Account Settings
4. Default is usually your profile timezone

**Example**: Set one account to Eastern (US stocks) and another to Tokyo (Nikkei futures).

## Timezone Impact on Market Hours

Your timezone affects displayed market hours:

**US Stock Market** (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET):
- Eastern: 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM
- Central: 8:30 AM - 3:00 PM
- Pacific: 6:30 AM - 1:00 PM

**Crypto Markets** (24/7):
- Always open regardless of timezone
- Times display in your selected timezone

## Verifying Your Timezone

To confirm your timezone is set correctly:

1. Check a recent trade in your journal
2. Compare the time displayed in TradeZella with your broker's time
3. Times should match (allowing for system delays)
4. If times don't match, update your timezone

## Changing Timezone Mid-Year

If you change your timezone:

- **Future Trades**: Recorded in the new timezone
- **Historical Trades**: May be recalculated and displayed in new timezone
- **Reports**: Times adjust to new timezone for consistency
- **Performance Data**: Should remain accurate (just displayed differently)

It's best to set your correct timezone when creating your account.

## System Default Timezone

TradeZella can use your system timezone:

1. Go to **Profile Settings**
2. Look for **"Use System Timezone"** toggle
3. Enable to automatically match your device timezone
4. Disable to manually set your timezone
5. Changes apply immediately

This is useful if you travel frequently or work across time zones.

## Timezone and International Trading

For international traders:

- **Set Your Work Timezone**: The timezone where you trade
- **Not Your Nationality**: Choose based on your trading schedule, not location
- **Consider Markets**: If trading US markets at night, set to US time anyway
- **Market Hours**: Always shows market hours in your timezone

**Example**: If you're in London trading US stocks at night, set timezone to Eastern for US market times.

## Troubleshooting Timezone Issues

**Times Still Wrong**:
- Clear your browser cache
- Log out and log back in
- Check your system timezone setting

**Market Hours Incorrect**:
- Verify you selected the right timezone
- Some markets have irregular hours (holidays, etc.)
- Check broker's market hours separately

**Import Times Off**:
- May be due to broker timezone mismatch
- You can edit imported trade times manually
- Ensure broker sync settings are correct
