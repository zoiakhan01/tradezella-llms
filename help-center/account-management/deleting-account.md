# Deleting an Account in TradeZella

Deleting a TradeZella Account permanently removes the account and all associated trade data from your workspace. This action cannot be undone, so ensure you've backed up any important data before proceeding.

## Important: Deletion is Permanent

When you delete a TradeZella Account:

- All trades in that account are permanently deleted
- Historical performance data is removed
- Account settings and preferences are lost
- Trade tags and notes associated with the account are deleted
- Import history for that account is removed
- This action cannot be reversed

Consider archiving your account instead if you want to keep the data but remove it from your active workspace.

## How to Delete an Account

### Step 1: Navigate to Account Settings

1. Click on the **"Accounts"** tab in the main navigation
2. Find the account you want to delete
3. Click the **three-dot menu** icon next to the account name
4. Select **"Account Settings"** or **"Settings"**

### Step 2: Locate Delete Option

1. In the Account Settings panel, scroll to the bottom
2. Look for the **"Danger Zone"** or **"Delete Account"** section
3. Click **"Delete This Account"**

### Step 3: Confirm Deletion

1. A confirmation dialog will appear warning about permanent deletion
2. You may be asked to type the account name to confirm
3. Review the warning message carefully
4. Click **"Yes, Delete Permanently"** to proceed

### Step 4: Deletion Complete

Once confirmed, the account is immediately removed from TradeZella. You will be redirected to your Accounts page, and the deleted account will no longer appear in your list.

## Before You Delete

Before deleting an account, consider:

- **Export Your Data**: Export trades or reports to CSV/PDF for your records
- **Screenshot Performance**: Capture important statistics or charts
- **Review Trades**: Make sure all trades have been recorded
- **Consider Archiving**: If you might need the data later, archive instead of delete

## Alternative: Archive Instead of Delete

If you want to keep your data but remove the account from your active workspace, consider **archiving** the account instead:

1. Go to Account Settings
2. Select **"Archive This Account"**
3. The account will be hidden but all data remains intact
4. You can unarchive later if needed

## Recovering After Deletion

There is no recovery option for deleted accounts. If you have permanently deleted an account by mistake, you would need to:

1. Manually recreate the account with the same settings
2. Re-import all trades from your broker or CSV files
3. Re-add any custom tags or notes

This is why backing up important data before deletion is recommended.
