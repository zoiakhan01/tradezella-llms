# Security Settings

Security Settings in TradeZella provides comprehensive tools to protect your account and trading data. You can manage your password, enable two-factor authentication, and monitor active sessions to ensure your account remains secure.

## Changing Your Password

It's recommended to update your password regularly to maintain account security:

1. Go to **Account Settings** > **Security Settings**
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter your current password for verification
4. Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters, with mixed case and numbers)
5. Confirm your new password
6. Click **"Update Password"**

Your new password will take effect immediately. You'll need to log in again on all devices using your new credentials.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Enable 2FA to add an extra layer of security to your account:

### Setting Up 2FA

1. Navigate to **Security Settings**
2. Click **"Enable Two-Factor Authentication"**
3. Download a authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, Microsoft Authenticator)
4. Scan the QR code displayed in TradeZella using your authenticator app
5. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator to verify setup
6. Save backup codes in a secure location

### Using 2FA

Once enabled, you'll be prompted for a 6-digit code from your authenticator app each time you log in. Always keep your backup codes in a safe place in case you lose access to your authenticator app.

## Session Management

Monitor and manage all active sessions across your devices:

1. Go to **Security Settings** > **Active Sessions**
2. View all devices currently logged into your TradeZella account
3. See the device type, location, and last access time for each session
4. Click **"Sign Out"** next to any session to remotely log out that device
5. Click **"Sign Out All Other Sessions"** to end all sessions except your current one

This feature helps you identify unauthorized access and maintain control over your account across devices.

## Login Alerts

Enable login notifications to receive alerts when your account is accessed:

- **New Login Notification**: Get alerted when logging in from a new device or location
- **Suspicious Activity**: Receive notifications about unusual login attempts
- **Failed Login Attempts**: Track unsuccessful login attempts to identify potential security issues
