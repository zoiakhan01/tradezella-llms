# Profile Settings

Your profile settings in TradeZella allow you to customize your account information and control how you interact with the platform. From your profile, you can update personal details, manage your avatar, adjust timezone settings, and configure notification preferences to suit your trading needs.

## Accessing Profile Settings

To access your profile settings, click on your avatar or username in the top-right corner of the TradeZella dashboard. Select "Profile Settings" from the dropdown menu. This will open the profile management page where you can modify various aspects of your account.

## Personal Information

### Updating Your Name

Your name appears on your account and in any shared reports or collaboration features. To update your name:

1. Navigate to the "Personal Information" section
2. Edit the first and last name fields
3. Click "Save Changes"

### Email Address

Your email address is used for login authentication and account recovery. To change your email:

1. Go to the "Email" field in Profile Settings
2. Enter your new email address
3. Check the verification email sent to your new address
4. Click the verification link to confirm the change

## Avatar and Display

Upload a profile avatar to personalize your account. You can use a photo, initials, or any image up to 5MB in size. This avatar will display in your profile and across the TradeZella platform.

## Timezone Configuration

Your timezone setting affects how trade times are displayed in your journal and reports. To change your timezone:

1. Locate the "Timezone" dropdown in Profile Settings
2. Select your current timezone from the list
3. All timestamps will automatically adjust to reflect your selection

## Notification Preferences

Manage how you receive alerts and updates from TradeZella:

- **Email Notifications**: Enable/disable email alerts for subscription updates, account alerts, or new features
- **In-App Notifications**: Toggle notifications that appear within TradeZella
- **Frequency**: Choose to receive instant notifications or daily digests
