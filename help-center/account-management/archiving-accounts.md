# Archiving Accounts in TradeZella

Archiving allows you to hide inactive trading accounts from your main workspace while preserving all historical data. This keeps your dashboard organized and clutter-free without permanently deleting important trading information.

## When to Archive an Account

Archive an account when you:

- Stop trading with a particular broker or strategy
- Want to organize multiple accounts but only focus on active ones
- Close a trading account but want to maintain historical records
- Need to clean up your workspace without losing data
- Temporarily pause trading on an account

## How to Archive an Account

### Step 1: Access Account Settings

1. Navigate to the **"Accounts"** section from the main menu
2. Find the account you want to archive
3. Click the **three-dot menu** icon next to the account name
4. Select **"Account Settings"**

### Step 2: Archive the Account

1. In the Account Settings panel, scroll down
2. Look for the **"Archive Account"** option
3. Click **"Archive This Account"**
4. A confirmation dialog will appear

### Step 3: Confirm Archival

1. Review the message explaining that data will be preserved
2. Click **"Yes, Archive This Account"** to proceed
3. The account will immediately be hidden from your active accounts list

## What Happens When You Archive

When you archive an account:

- It's removed from your active Accounts view
- All trade data, performance metrics, and history are preserved
- Account settings remain intact
- You can still access archived accounts through a separate view
- The account will not appear in your dashboard summary or analytics
- Tags and notes associated with trades remain saved

## Accessing Archived Accounts

To view or interact with archived accounts:

1. Go to the **"Accounts"** section
2. Look for a **"Show Archived"** option or toggle
3. Enable the view to display archived accounts
4. Archived accounts will appear in a separate section

## Unarchiving an Account

If you need to return an archived account to active status:

1. View your archived accounts (using "Show Archived")
2. Find the account you want to unarchive
3. Click the **three-dot menu** next to the account
4. Select **"Unarchive Account"**
5. The account immediately returns to your active Accounts list

## Viewing Archived Data

Even while archived, you can still:

- View historical trades and performance reports
- Export trade data for record-keeping
- Review performance analytics
- Access account statistics and reports

Simply access the archived accounts view and select the account to review its data.

## Deleting vs. Archiving

- **Archive**: Hides the account but keeps all data - use for accounts you might need later
- **Delete**: Permanently removes all data - use only if you're certain you won't need the information

We recommend archiving rather than deleting unless you have a specific reason to permanently remove the data.
