# Getting Started with Prop Firm Sync

TradeZella's Prop Firm Sync provides a unified dashboard for tracking all your prop firm finances, accounts, and performance in one place.

## Connect Your Bank Account

Use Plaid integration to automatically connect your bank account. Once connected, TradeZella will automatically detect and categorize transactions including purchases, resets, fees, and payouts. Alternatively, you can track your prop firm activity manually if you prefer not to connect your bank.

## Track Your Spending

View your spending organized by firm, challenge type, and challenge size. Get a complete picture of your net total spending and return on investment (ROI) across all your prop firm accounts.

## Manage Your Challenges

Create new challenges, connect them to your trading accounts, and monitor your account balance and rules compliance in real time. The dashboard gives you immediate visibility into how you're performing against each challenge's requirements.
