# Challenge Simulator (Beta)

The Challenge Simulator is a beta feature that lets you test your trading against prop firm challenge rules before you spend real money on evaluations.

## Test Before You Trade

Run simulations using your actual trade history to project how you would perform against specific challenge configurations. This helps you understand whether your trading style is a good match for different challenge rules and requirements.

## Eliminate Wasted Evaluations

By simulating your performance first, you can identify mismatches between your trading approach and challenge rules. This means fewer failed evaluations and less wasted capital on challenges that aren't suited to your style.

## Optimize Your Strategy

Use the simulator as a self-improvement tool to adjust your trading decisions before committing to a real challenge. Match your trading style to challenge configurations that give you the highest probability of success.
