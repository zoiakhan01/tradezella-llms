# Prop Firm Performance Analytics

Gain deep insights into your prop firm performance with detailed analytics that help you identify strengths and areas for improvement.

## Pass Rates and Success Metrics

View your pass rate broken down by firm and by account type. Understand which firms and challenge types are your strongest areas and where you need to focus your efforts.

## Failure Analysis

When accounts don't pass, analyze the specific reasons for failure including daily loss violations, maximum drawdown breaches, and consistency issues. Understanding failure reasons is critical for improving your approach.

## Performance Rankings

Identify your best performing firm and challenge combination. Discover which prop firms get you to payout fastest, allowing you to focus your capital on the most productive opportunities.

## Trade-Level Insights

Failed accounts link directly to the causative trades that led to the failure. This connection to your trading journal enables powerful learning opportunities—you can review exactly what went wrong and adjust your trading strategy accordingly.
