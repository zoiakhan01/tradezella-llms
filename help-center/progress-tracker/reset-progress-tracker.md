# How to Reset Your Progress Tracker

The Progress Tracker in TradeZella helps you maintain accountability toward your trading goals. When you're ready to set new objectives or start a fresh tracking period, you can reset your progress tracker easily.

## When to Reset Your Progress Tracker

Consider resetting when:

- You're starting a new trading month, week, or period
- You've achieved your current goals and want new ones
- You want to eliminate previous tracking data
- You're adjusting your goals for improved performance
- You're implementing new trading strategies
- You're starting a new trading challenge or competition

## How to Reset Your Progress Tracker

To reset your progress tracker:

1. Log in to your TradeZella account
2. Navigate to the Progress Tracker section
3. Click on "Settings" or the Menu button (three dots)
4. Look for "Reset Progress Tracker" or "Reset Data"
5. Select the reset option
6. Review what will be reset in the confirmation dialog
7. Confirm the reset action

## What Gets Reset

When you reset your progress tracker, the following data resets:

- **Goal Progress**: Daily and periodic goal tracking counters return to zero
- **Streak Counter**: Trading streak resets to zero days
- **Historical Progress Data**: Previous period data is archived or removed
- **Daily Metrics**: Daily P&L, win rate, and trade count reset for the new period

## What Does NOT Get Reset

Your historical data remains intact:

- **Trade History**: All your trades remain in your account
- **Strategy Data**: Your strategies and their performance history
- **Notebook**: Your notes and analysis
- **Reports**: Historical reports and analytics
- **Account Information**: Your profile and account settings

## Setting New Goals After Reset

Once you've reset your progress tracker:

1. Establish new realistic goals aligned with your trading plan
2. Set daily P&L targets or win rate objectives
3. Define streak goals (consecutive profitable days)
4. Set performance benchmarks for the period
5. Consider risk management thresholds

## Archiving Previous Tracking Data

Before resetting, you may want to preserve previous data:

1. Take screenshots of your progress tracker
2. Export reports from your trading period
3. Document your achievements and lessons learned
4. Save goal achievement data in your Notebook
5. Review your period results before resetting

## Viewing Historical Progress

After resetting, access previous tracking periods:

1. Navigate to Reports section
2. Select "Historical Performance" or "Period Analysis"
3. Choose the date range for previous tracking period
4. View your performance data from before the reset

## Partial Reset Options

Some TradeZella plans allow partial resets:

- Reset daily metrics while preserving monthly progress
- Clear specific goals while keeping others
- Archive old data instead of deleting it
- Customize which data elements to reset

Check your reset options in the settings menu.

## Resetting Multiple Trackers

If you track different metrics:

1. You can reset individual metric trackers separately
2. Reset daily tracker independent of weekly/monthly
3. Maintain some metrics while resetting others
4. Customize reset schedule per metric

## Tips for Effective Goal Setting

After resetting your progress tracker:

- Set SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
- Break large goals into smaller daily targets
- Review and adjust goals weekly
- Use your previous period results to inform new targets
- Document your goals in the Notebook for reference

## Recovering from Accidental Reset

If you accidentally reset your tracker:

1. Contact TradeZella support immediately
2. Provide details about what was reset
3. Support may be able to restore data from backups
4. The sooner you reach out, the better chance of recovery
