# Getting Started with the Progress Tracker

The Progress Tracker is your tool for building trading discipline by consistently following daily habits and trading rules.

## Track Your Daily Rules

Set up both mandatory and custom rules that guide your daily trading activities. Each day, check off the rules you've completed to maintain accountability and visibility into your habits.

## Monitor Your Compliance

Track your follow rate percentage over time to see how consistently you're adhering to your rules. This metric reveals patterns in your discipline and highlights which rules you're strongest at maintaining.

## Dashboard Visibility

A dashboard widget gives you quick access to your compliance status, making it easy to see at a glance how well you're following your trading rules today.

## Identify Your Strengths

By tracking which rules you follow consistently, you can identify your strongest habits and build on them. Conversely, you can spot which rules need more attention and focus your discipline efforts accordingly.
