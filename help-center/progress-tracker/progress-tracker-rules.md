# Setting Up Progress Tracker Rules

Configure the rules that will help you maintain trading discipline and build consistent habits.

## Mandatory Rules

Mandatory rules must be checked off every day. These are the non-negotiable habits that form the foundation of your trading discipline. All users have mandatory rules that align with best trading practices.

## Custom Rules

Create custom rules tailored to your specific trading approach and goals. These allow you to track habits and behaviors unique to your trading strategy.

## Example Rules

Consider tracking habits such as:

- Reviewed pre-market plan
- Set stop loss on every trade
- Followed position sizing guidelines
- Journaled all trades
- Reviewed daily performance

## Managing Your Rules

You have full control over your rules configuration. Edit existing rules, delete ones that no longer serve you, and reorder them to match your priorities. When needed, reset your rule tracking to start fresh.
