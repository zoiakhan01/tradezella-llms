# Can I Utilize TradeZella for Backtesting Even Without a Broker?

Yes! TradeZella backtesting works completely independently of broker connections. You can create, execute, and analyze backtesting sessions without connecting to any brokerage account.

## Why Backtesting Doesn't Require a Broker

TradeZella's backtesting module uses historical market data to simulate trading, not live broker connections. This means:

- **No broker needed**: Complete backtesting access without any broker account
- **No trading capital required**: Simulate trades without risking real money
- **Independent platform**: Backtesting operates separately from live trading
- **Learning-friendly**: Perfect for developing and testing strategies before trading live

## Getting Started with Backtesting (No Broker)

### Step 1: Sign Up for TradeZella

Create your TradeZella account:
1. Visit the TradeZella website
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email address
5. You now have immediate access to backtesting

**No broker connection required** - You can start backtesting today.

### Step 2: Choose Your Plan

Select a plan that fits your needs:
- **Free Plan**: Limited backtesting (1 active session, 5 total)
- **Essential Plan**: More sessions (3 active, 50 total), 5-year data
- **Pro Plan**: Unlimited sessions, 20+ years of data

Broker connections are never required to use any plan.

### Step 3: Create Your First Session

1. Click **Backtesting** in the main menu
2. Select **Create New Session**
3. Choose your symbol (ES, EURUSD, AAPL, BTC, etc.)
4. Select your timeframe
5. Set your date range
6. Start backtesting immediately

Trade like you would live, but with historical data:

### Complete Performance Analysis

- Analyze R-multiples and risk-reward

- Identify best performing setups

## When to Connect a Broker

You only need a broker connection for:
- **Live trading**: Actually placing real trades with capital
- **Trade import**: Importing real trades for analysis and journaling
- **Performance tracking**: Comparing live results to backtesting
- **Prop firm sync**: Integrating with prop firm data

But these are entirely optional. Backtesting works perfectly without them.

## Trading Journal Without a Broker

Even without a broker connection, your backtested trades auto-journal:
- Every backtested trade is captured in your journal
- Full performance metrics calculated
- Charts saved with trade setups
- Notes and observations recorded
- Historical record maintained

Use your journal to:
- Track backtesting improvement over time
- Identify your best performing setups
- Review and learn from past simulations
- Build a trading playbook
- Develop confidence in your strategies

Broker-independent backtesting means:

## Upgrading to Live Trading Later

When ready to trade live:
1. Connect a broker to TradeZella
2. Import your trading account
3. Begin placing real trades
4. Leverage your backtested strategies
5. Compare live performance to backtesting

Your backtesting sessions remain in your account for reference.
