# Using Indicators in Backtesting

Enhance your strategy validation with comprehensive technical and ICT indicators.

## Available Technical Indicators

TradeZella includes a full suite of standard technical indicators:

### Trend Indicators

- **Moving Averages** - Simple (SMA) and Exponential (EMA) for trend identification
- **MACD** - For momentum and trend confirmation

### Momentum Indicators

- **RSI (Relative Strength Index)** - Identify overbought/oversold conditions
- **Volume** - Analyze trading volume patterns

### Volatility Indicators

- **Bollinger Bands** - Measure volatility and identify price extremes

### Market Profile Indicators

- **VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price)** - Reference for institutional price levels

## ICT Indicators

Specialized indicators for ICT (Inner Circle Trader) methodologies:

- **Fair Value Gaps (FVG)** - Identify price imbalances
- **Order Blocks** - Recognize support and resistance zones
- **Liquidity Levels** - Pinpoint areas of interest and manipulation

## Customization Options

Full control over your indicator setup:

- **Adjust parameters** for each indicator to match your strategy
- **Customize colors** for better visual identification
- **Display flexibility** - Show or hide indicators as needed

## Multiple Indicators

Combine multiple indicators simultaneously to:

- Validate strategy signals across different analysis methods
- Create more robust entry and exit criteria
- Better understand market dynamics during your backtest
