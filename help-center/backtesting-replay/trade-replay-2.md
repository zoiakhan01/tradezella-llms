# Trade Replay 2.0 - Analyzing Trades Second by Second

Trade Replay 2.0 is TradeZella's advanced tool for analyzing your past trades with incredible detail. Watch your executed trades unfold in real-time, second by second, to understand exactly what happened and how to improve.

## What is Trade Replay 2.0?

### Understanding Trade Replay

Trade Replay 2.0 allows you to:
- **Re-experience trades**: Watch exactly how your trade played out
- **Analyze decisions**: See if your entry/exit made sense in real-time
- **Learn from mistakes**: Understand what went wrong or right
- **Improve techniques**: Refine your decision-making process
- **Build confidence**: Confirm your trading logic was sound

### Key Features

- **Second-by-second playback**: Granular detail of trade execution
- **Integrated journaling**: Document observations while reviewing
- **Speed controls**: Play at 1x, 2x, 5x, or 10x speed
- **Chart annotations**: Mark key moments
- **Performance overlay**: See trade metrics in real-time
- **Share capability**: Send replay to mentors or trading partners

## Accessing Trade Replay 2.0

### Starting a Trade Replay

**From Your Journal:**
1. Navigate to **Journal** section
2. Find the trade you want to replay
3. Click the trade row
4. Click **"Replay Trade"** button
5. Trade Replay 2.0 opens in new window

**From Backtesting Session:**
1. Complete backtesting session
2. Right-click completed trade
3. Select **"Replay This Trade"**
4. Replay window launches
5. Trade unfolds from your entry

**From Broker Import:**
1. View imported live trades
2. Click on trade you want to analyze
3. Select **"Trade Replay"**
4. Automatically loads your actual execution
5. Analyzes real market data at trade time

### Importing Real Trades

To replay actual executed trades:
1. Connect your broker to TradeZella
2. Trades import automatically
3. Select trade in journal
4. Click **Replay Trade**
5. Actual market replay loads

## Replaying Trades Step-by-Step

### Starting the Replay

1. **Trade Replay 2.0 window opens**
2. **Chart shows entry candle**
3. **Entry point marked** (green for long, red for short)
4. **Your entry price highlighted**
5. **Ready to play**

### Playing Through Your Trade

**Basic Playback:**
1. Click **Play** or press **Spacebar**
2. Market price unfolds candle by candle
3. Watch your position value change
4. See when you could have exited
5. Observe price action around your trade

**Controlling Playback:**
- **Pause**: Stop at any point (Spacebar)
- **Slow motion**: 0.5x or 1x speed for detail
- **Fast forward**: 5x or 10x for quick review
- **Frame by frame**: Arrow keys for precision

### Marking Important Moments

During replay, mark key points:
1. **Click marker button** at critical moment
2. Add note about what's happening
3. "This is where I should have exited"
4. "Price confirmed at support"
5. Build annotated trading history

## Analyzing Different Trade Types

### Winning Trades Analysis

For profitable trades:
1. **Watch the setup**: How did entry develop?
2. **Confirm decision**: Was entry justified?
3. **Identify key moments**: When did trade turn profitable?
4. **Note success factors**: What made this trade work?
5. **Document pattern**: Capture setup for playbook

**Questions to Answer:**
- Did you enter at optimal point?
- Was your stop appropriately placed?
- Could you have made more profit?
- Should you have exited earlier?

### Losing Trades Analysis

For losing trades:
1. **Understand entry**: Why did you enter?
2. **Identify problem**: What went wrong?
3. **Analyze market context**: Was setup invalid?
4. **Review stop placement**: Did stop protect?
5. **Learn lesson**: What will you do differently?

**Questions to Answer:**
- Was this a bad setup or bad luck?
- Should you have entered at all?
- Was your stop too tight?
- Did you exit too early?

### Break-Even Trades

For trades that closed near entry:
1. **Analyze near-misses**: How close to profit?
2. **Identify obstacles**: What prevented profit?
3. **Explore alternatives**: Different entry would help?
4. **Review timing**: Timing issue or setup issue?
5. **Plan adjustments**: How to turn these into wins?

## Replay Speed Settings

### Recommended Speeds

**Detailed Analysis (0.5x or 1x)**
- Best for: Learning price action
- When: Analyzing significant trades
- Time: Takes longer, but very detailed
- Value: High learning potential

**Normal Playback (2x)**
- Best for: Balanced analysis
- When: Regular trade review
- Time: Reasonable duration
- Value: Good detail, reasonable time

**Quick Review (5x)**
- Best for: Scanning multiple trades
- When: Looking for patterns
- Time: Quick process
- Value: Good for volume review

**Summary View (10x)**
- Best for: Getting overview only
- When: Rapid assessment
- Time: Very fast
- Value: Good for big picture

## Limitations and Considerations

### Historical Data Quality

Trade replay accuracy depends on:
- **Data availability**: How much tick data exists?
- **Broker data**: Import quality varies
- **Market conditions**: Volatile periods have more variation
- **Timeframe**: Lower timeframes need more data

### Backtested vs Real Trades

Differences you might notice:
- **Slippage**: Real trades have it, backtested might not perfectly simulate
- **Speed**: Real fills vary by broker, slippage differences
- **Market data**: Slight variations in historical feeds
- **Psychological impact**: Real trades feel different than backtested

### Technical Considerations

- **Requires good internet**: Stream replay data smoothly
- **Processing power**: Fast computer speeds up replay
- **Data storage**: Store replay videos locally if desired
- **Privacy**: Your trades stay private unless shared
