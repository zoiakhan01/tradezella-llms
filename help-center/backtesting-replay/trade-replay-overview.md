# Getting Started with Trade Replay

Review and analyze your actual trades with second-by-second precision to improve your decision-making process.

## What is Trade Replay?

Trade Replay is a powerful analysis tool that allows you to replay any trade from your trading history tick-by-tick. This feature helps you understand your entry, exit, and execution decisions in detail and identify opportunities for improvement.

## Key Features

### Precision Analysis

- **Tick-by-tick replay** for detailed price action review
- **Second-by-second precision** to understand exact execution timing
- **Chart integration** to see actual price movement during your trade

### Integrated Journaling

- **Journal while replaying** to capture real-time insights
- Add notes about what you could have done differently
- Document key price levels and decision points
- Create comprehensive trade reviews for future reference

### Execution Review

- **See actual executions** on the chart
- Analyze how price moved relative to your entry and exit
- Understand slippage and execution quality
- Review order fills and timing

## Accessing Trade Replay

Trade Replay is available for **Pro plan users**:

1. Navigate to your **Trade page**
2. Select the trade you want to analyze
3. Click the **Replay button**
4. The trade will replay tick-by-tick on your chart

## Improvement Analysis

Use Trade Replay to:

- Identify patterns in your trading decisions
- Recognize missed opportunities
- Analyze what could have been done differently
- Refine your entry and exit strategies
- Build confidence in proven strategies
