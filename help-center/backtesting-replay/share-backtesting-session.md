# Is It Possible to Share My Backtesting Session with Another Trader?

Yes! TradeZella allows you to share your backtesting sessions with other traders on the platform, enabling collaborative analysis and learning opportunities.

## How to Share a Backtesting Session

### Step 1: Open Your Session

Navigate to the **Backtesting** module and locate the session you want to share. Click on it to open the session details view.

### Step 2: Access the Share Menu

Look for the **"Share"** button (usually located in the top toolbar or session header). Click it to open the sharing options.

### Step 3: Select Sharing Options

You'll see several sharing preferences:

**Who to Share With**
- Share with specific TradeZella users (enter their email addresses or usernames)
- Make the session accessible to your team or group
- Set the session as "Read-only" or "View-only" to prevent modifications

**Share Scope**
- Full session access: Others can view the session, trades, and analysis
- Chart only: Share just the chart without trade details
- Summary view: Share performance metrics and statistics only

### Step 4: Generate Share Link

TradeZella will generate a unique shareable link for your session. You can:
- Copy the direct link to share via email, Slack, or messaging apps
- Share the link with anyone you choose
- Revoke access at any time

## What Others Can See

When you share a backtesting session, other traders can view:
- Your trade entries and exits
- Order types and quantities
- Entry and exit reasons (if documented in notes)
- Performance metrics and P&L
- Charts with all technical indicators
- Your journal notes and trade analysis

## Setting Permissions

Control what shared traders can do with your session:

**View-Only Access**
- Others can watch and analyze your trading
- Cannot modify trades or add their own trades
- Cannot download session data
- Best for mentor/mentee relationships

**Collaborative Access**
- Available on Pro plans
- Others can add annotations and notes
- Real-time discussion capability
- Perfect for trading groups and teams

## Privacy and Security

- Shared sessions do not expose your account or personal information
- Only the specific session data is accessible
- You maintain control of all sharing permissions
- Session links can be revoked instantly

## Use Cases for Session Sharing

1. **Mentorship**: Share sessions with a trading mentor for feedback
2. **Peer learning**: Compare strategies with other traders
3. **Team analysis**: Collaborate with your trading group
4. **Review trading**: Have experienced traders critique your decisions
5. **Teaching**: Instructors can share example trades with students

## Revoking Access

To stop sharing a session:
1. Open the session
2. Click **Share** in the menu
3. Select the user or link you want to revoke
4. Click **Remove Access**

Access is immediately revoked and the shared link becomes inactive.
