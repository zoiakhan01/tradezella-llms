Validate your forex strategies with comprehensive historical data and forex-specific tools.

TradeZella handles all forex-specific metrics automatically:

### Pip-Based Calculations

- Accurate pip value calculations for all pairs

Leverage specialized indicators designed for forex analysis:

- **Fair Value Gaps** - Identify price imbalances in forex pairs
- **Order Blocks** - Recognize institutional order placement zones
- **Liquidity Levels** - Find areas where institutions hunt liquidity

Test your forex strategies against **11+ years of historical data** to:

- Validate trading setups across multiple market cycles
- Understand strategy performance in trending and ranging markets
- Measure consistency and risk-adjusted returns
