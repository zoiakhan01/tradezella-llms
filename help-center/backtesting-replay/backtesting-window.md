# Understanding the Backtesting Window

Get familiar with the comprehensive backtesting interface and its key components.

## Interface Layout

The backtesting window is organized into several functional areas:

### Chart Area

- **Candlestick charts** displaying price action in your selected timeframe
- Full view of market conditions during your test period
- Visual reference for analyzing entry and exit points

### Timeframe Selector

- Quickly switch between different timeframes
- Adjust your view from 1-minute to monthly intervals
- Maintain continuity in your strategy testing

### Drawing Tools

- Technical analysis tools for marking key levels
- Annotation capabilities for documenting trade analysis
- Visual markers for support, resistance, and trend identification

## Trading Controls

### Order Entry Panel

Place orders with full flexibility:

- **Market orders** for immediate execution simulation
- **Limit orders** for specific price targets
- **Stop orders** for risk management

### Trade Management Panel

Monitor and manage active positions:

- View open trades and their current performance
- Adjust position sizes and risk parameters
- Close positions at any time during the test

### Session Information

Track important metrics:

- Current date and time in the backtest
- Account balance and equity
- Active positions and pending orders

## Navigation Controls

Control the pace of your backtesting experience:

- **Step forward bar-by-bar** to analyze specific candles in detail
- **Play at various speeds** to move through data faster
- Pause and resume testing as needed for analysis
