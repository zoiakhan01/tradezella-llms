# How to Create a Backtesting Session

Creating a backtesting session in TradeZella is straightforward and allows you to practice trading strategies on historical market data. Follow these steps to get started with your first backtesting session.

## Step 1: Navigate to the Backtesting Module

From your TradeZella dashboard, click on the **Backtesting** tab in the main navigation menu. This will take you to the backtesting hub where you can view all your previous sessions and create new ones.

## Step 2: Click "Create New Session"

Look for the **"Create New Session"** button (typically located at the top right of the backtesting interface). Click this button to initiate the session creation wizard.

## Step 3: Select Your Symbol

In the symbol field, enter or select the instrument you want to backtest. TradeZella supports:
- Forex pairs (EURUSD, GBPUSD, etc.)
- Futures contracts (ES, NQ, CL, GC)
- Stocks (Apple, Tesla, etc.)
- Cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.)

## Step 4: Choose Your Timeframe

Select the timeframe for your backtesting session. Available options range from 1-minute to monthly candles. Common choices are:
- 1-minute, 5-minute, 15-minute (scalping strategies)
- 1-hour, 4-hour (swing trading)
- Daily, weekly, monthly (position trading)

## Step 5: Set the Date Range

Specify the start and end dates for your backtesting period. You can select any historical date range supported by TradeZella's data coverage. Use the calendar picker or type dates directly in MM/DD/YYYY format.

## Step 6: Configure Chart Settings (Optional)

Customize your backtesting environment:
- **Chart type**: Candlestick, OHLC, or line charts
- **Indicators**: Add technical indicators to your chart
- **Grid and volume**: Toggle grid lines and volume display
- **Dark/Light mode**: Choose your preferred color scheme

## Step 7: Create and Launch

Once all parameters are configured, click **"Create Session"** to initialize your backtesting environment. The system will load historical data and display the chart with your symbol at the specified timeframe and date range.

## Tips for Success

- Start with shorter date ranges (1-2 months) to focus on specific market conditions
- Use familiar timeframes that match your trading style
- Add only essential indicators to avoid chart clutter
- Save your session frequently for later review

Your new backtesting session is now ready for trading simulation. You can begin placing trades directly on the chart.
