# Getting Started with Backtesting

Validate and refine your trading strategies by testing them against 11+ years of historical market data.

## What is Backtesting?

Backtesting allows you to simulate trades based on your strategy rules using past market conditions. This powerful feature helps you understand strategy performance before risking real capital.

## Creating a Backtesting Session

### Step 1: Set Up Your Session

1. Select your desired **symbol** (currency pair, futures contract, stock, or cryptocurrency)
2. Choose your preferred **timeframe** (1-minute to monthly)
3. Define your **date range** to test specific market periods

### Step 2: Execute Trades

1. Use the **buy/sell buttons** to place simulated trades
2. Choose from **market, limit, or stop orders**
3. Manage your positions through the trade management panel

## Supported Markets

TradeZella backtesting supports multiple asset classes:

- **Forex** - Full currency pair selection
- **Futures** - All major contracts
- **Stocks** - Comprehensive coverage
- **Cryptocurrency** - Leading digital assets

## Trade Journaling and Analysis

All trades placed during backtesting are automatically journaled, giving you a complete record for analysis. Utilize multiple chart layouts and indicators to enhance your testing environment.
