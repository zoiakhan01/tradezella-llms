# Backtesting Data Availability

TradeZella provides extensive historical data coverage across multiple markets and timeframes.

## Historical Data Coverage

You have access to **11+ years of historical market data**, allowing you to test strategies across various market conditions and cycles.

## Supported Markets and Instruments

### Forex

- **100+ currency pairs** including:
  - Major pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, etc.)
  - Minor pairs (EUR/GBP, AUD/USD, etc.)
  - Exotic pairs for specialized strategies

### Futures

- **Major contracts**: ES (E-mini S&P 500), NQ (Nasdaq 100), YM (Dow Jones), CL (Crude Oil), GC (Gold)
- **Micro contracts** for lower capital requirements
- Full tick-by-tick data for precise analysis

### Stocks

Comprehensive coverage of major equities and market segments

### Cryptocurrency

Leading digital assets for modern traders

## Available Timeframes

Test your strategies across multiple timeframes:

- **1-minute** for scalping strategies
- **5-minute, 15-minute, 30-minute** for short-term trading
- **Hourly, 4-hour** for day trading and swing trading
- **Daily, weekly, monthly** for long-term analysis

## Data Quality

All data is sourced from reliable market feeds and validated for accuracy to ensure meaningful backtest results.
