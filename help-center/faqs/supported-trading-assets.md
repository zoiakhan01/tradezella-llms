# What Trading Assets Are Supported?

TradeZella is designed to work with traders across multiple asset classes, providing comprehensive support for stocks, options, forex, futures, and cryptocurrency. Whether you're a day trader focused on equities, a forex specialist, or a diversified trader managing multiple asset types, TradeZella allows you to track and analyze all your trades in one unified platform. Each asset class has specific handling and calculation methods to ensure accurate performance metrics and risk analysis.

The platform automatically recognizes asset types when you import trades from your broker, whether through direct integrations, API connections, or manual CSV uploads. This flexibility means you can consolidate your trading activity across multiple brokers and asset classes without worrying about compatibility issues. TradeZella's analytics engine is specifically tuned to handle the unique characteristics of each asset type, from contract specifications in futures to multipliers in options trading.

Understanding which assets you can track in TradeZella helps you make the most of the platform's features. The system provides detailed performance metrics, risk calculations, and portfolio analysis tailored to each asset class's unique characteristics and market conventions.

## Supported Asset Classes

### Equities (Stocks)
- US stocks (NYSE, NASDAQ, OTCBB)
- International stocks (TSX, FTSE, ASX, etc.)
- Fractional shares
- Stock dividends and corporate actions
- Detailed commission and fee tracking per trade

### Options
- Call and put options
- Single leg and multi-leg strategies (spreads, straddles, etc.)
- Defined risk and undefined risk positions
- Expiration date and strike price tracking
- IV (Implied Volatility) impact analysis
- Theta decay calculations

### Forex
- Major currency pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, etc.)
- Minor and exotic pairs
- Pip-based profit/loss calculations
- Leverage and margin requirements
- Lot size conversions for position sizing analysis

### Futures
- Index futures (ES, NQ, YM, etc.)
- Currency futures
- Commodity futures
- Contract multiplier calculations
- Expiration tracking and rollover handling
- Spread trading (calendar spreads, inter-commodity)

### Cryptocurrency
- Bitcoin, Ethereum, and altcoins
- Spot trading
- Futures contracts on crypto exchanges
- Staking and yield rewards tracking
- Multi-exchange import support (Binance, Kraken, Coinbase, etc.)

## Asset-Specific Features

**Commission & Fee Handling:** TradeZella accurately calculates profit/loss accounting for commissions, spreads, and fees specific to each asset class and broker.

**Position Sizing:** The platform accounts for contract multipliers, pip values, and lot sizes when calculating position metrics and risk exposure.

**Tax Reporting:** Assets are categorized appropriately for tax purposes, with support for wash sale detection and different holding period classifications.
