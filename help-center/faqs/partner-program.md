# TradeZella Partner Program

TradeZella's Partner Program offers an opportunity to earn recurring commissions by referring traders and trading communities to our platform. Whether you're a content creator, trading educator, community manager, or simply someone with influence in trading circles, you can generate meaningful passive income by sharing TradeZella with your audience. The program is designed to reward partners for bringing high-quality traders to the platform while maintaining alignment with TradeZella's values.

The Partner Program includes competitive commission rates, detailed performance tracking, timely payouts, and dedicated partner support. Unlike many affiliate programs that pay one-time commissions, TradeZella offers recurring revenue sharing, meaning you earn commissions as long as your referred customers remain active users. This creates strong earning potential for partners who genuinely believe in the product and promote it authentically to engaged communities.

## Program Overview

### Key Features

**Recurring Commission Model:** Earn monthly commissions on referred customers' subscriptions, not just one-time sign-up bonuses. As long as your referral remains a paying customer, you continue earning.

**Competitive Commission Rates:**
- **Standard Tier:** 20% recurring commission per referred customer
- **Premium Tier:** 25% recurring commission (after 25+ active referrals)
- **Elite Tier:** 30% recurring commission (after 100+ active referrals)

**Marketing Support:** TradeZella provides partners with promotional materials, graphics, landing pages, and content templates to make promotion easier.

**Dedicated Partner Manager:** Premium tier partners receive a dedicated account manager to help optimize promotions and growth strategies.

**Real-time Tracking Dashboard:** Track referrals, sign-ups, active customers, and commissions earned through an intuitive partner dashboard.

**Monthly Payouts:** Commissions are calculated monthly and paid out via your preferred method (bank transfer, PayPal, cryptocurrency).

**No Caps:** Unlimited earning potential with no monthly commission caps or maximum referral limits.

## Commission Structure Details

### How Commissions Are Calculated

**Monthly Recurring:** For each referred customer with active subscription in a given month, you earn the commission percentage of their monthly subscription fee.

**Example:**
- Customer referred: John (Standard subscription at $49/month)
- Your commission rate: 20% (Standard Tier)
- Monthly commission: $49 × 0.20 = $9.80 per month
- After 12 months: $117.60 earned from John's subscription alone

**Example Over Time:**
- Month 1: Refer 5 customers at $49/month each = $49 × 0.20 × 5 = $49 earned
- Month 2: Same 5 customers stay + 3 new = $49 × 0.20 × 8 = $78.40 earned
- Month 6: Building 30+ active referrals = $49 × 0.20 × 30+ = $294+ earned per month

### Tier Advancement

**Standard Tier (Default)**
- 20% commission rate
- Automatic upon program approval
- No minimum referrals required

**Premium Tier**
- 25% commission rate
- Unlocked after 25+ active referrals in good standing
- Qualifies for dedicated partner manager
- Higher-tier promotional support

**Elite Tier**
- 30% commission rate
- Unlocked after 100+ active referrals in good standing
- Priority support and custom partnership opportunities
- Potential for exclusive bonus structures

### Commission Eligibility Rules

**Valid Referrals:** Referral must sign up through your unique link, create account, and complete email verification.

**Activation:** Commission begins once customer completes first month of paid subscription (not trial).

**Retention:** You only earn commission while customer maintains active subscription. Cancelled subscriptions stop earning.

**Annual Recalculation:** Tier status reviewed annually. You may advance to higher tiers or drop if referrals become inactive.

**Fraud Prevention:** Fake signups, bot accounts, or suspicious activity may result in disqualification and commission clawback.

## Common Questions

### How Much Can I Earn?
Earnings depend on audience size, engagement, and conversion rate. A partner with 50,000 engaged followers typically converts 2-5% to paying users. At 5% conversion of 50,000 = 2,500 referrals × $49/month × 20% = $24,500 monthly. Of course, most partners will see lower numbers initially and grow over time.

### When Do I Get Paid?
Commissions are calculated and paid the first Friday of each month. Payments are processed to your selected method (bank transfer typically takes 3-5 business days).

### What If a Customer Cancels?
Once a customer cancels their subscription, you no longer earn commission on them. However, if they reactivate later, commissions resume if they came through your referral link.

### Can I Promote to Multiple Audiences?
Yes. Many partners promote across multiple platforms (YouTube, Twitter, blog, newsletter, etc.). All referrals through your link count toward your referral total.

### Is There a Maximum I Can Earn?
No. Unlimited earning potential with no commission caps.

### Can I Earn Commission on My Own Referral?
If you refer yourself, you don't earn commission on your own subscription. However, you can create additional accounts through different emails; consult with partner support about this scenario.
