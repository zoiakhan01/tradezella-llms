# Our Pricing

Choose the TradeZella plan that best fits your trading needs.

## Basic Plan

**$29/month** or **$24/month** (billed annually)

- 1 active account
- Core trade tracking features
- Performance analytics
- Basic reporting

## Pro Plan

**$49/month** or **$33.25/month** (billed annually)

- Unlimited active accounts
- All Basic Plan features plus:
- Trade Replay functionality
- Advanced Backtesting
- 5 GB storage
- Unlimited Playbooks

## Choose Your Billing Frequency

Pay month-to-month for flexibility, or commit to annual billing and save approximately 17% on both plans.

## Upgrade or Downgrade

You can change your plan or billing frequency at any time through your subscription settings. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades apply to your next billing cycle with prorated credits available for future billing periods.
