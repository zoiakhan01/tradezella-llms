# How to Cancel or Switch Your Subscription

Manage your TradeZella subscription with flexibility and ease.

## Cancel Your Subscription

To cancel your subscription, go to your Subscription settings and select "Cancel My Subscription." Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle, giving you time to download or export any important data.

## Switch to a Different Plan

Change your plan directly in your subscription settings. You can upgrade to a higher tier or downgrade to a lower tier at any time.

## Upgrades

Upgrading to a higher-tier plan takes effect immediately. You'll be charged the difference on a prorated basis for the remainder of your billing cycle.

## Downgrades

When you downgrade to a lower-tier plan, the change applies to your next billing cycle. No refund is issued for your current billing period, but you'll receive a prorated credit applied to your future billing.

## Use Coupons During Switches

If you have a coupon code, you can apply it when switching plans to receive discounts on your new subscription.
