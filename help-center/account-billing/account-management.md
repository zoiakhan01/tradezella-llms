# Account Management in TradeZella

Manage all aspects of your trading accounts within TradeZella, from creation to deletion and everything in between.

## Add New Accounts

Create new trading accounts to track different brokers, strategies, or prop firm challenges. Each account maintains its own trade history and performance metrics.

## Delete Accounts

Remove accounts you no longer need. Note that account deletion is irreversible, so ensure you've backed up any important data before proceeding.

## Adjust Account Settings

Modify account details including balances and historical data. You can adjust your account balance to correct for transfers or starting capital changes, and clear trades if needed.

## Archive Accounts

Archive accounts that you want to keep for historical reference but don't want to count toward your account limits. Archived accounts won't be included in your dashboard summaries or account limits. Note that archived accounts cannot be unarchived.

## Transfer Account Data

Move data between accounts to reorganize your tracking structure as your trading needs evolve.

## Account Limits by Plan

Your TradeZella plan determines how many active accounts you can maintain:

- **Basic Plan**: 1 active account
- **Pro Plan**: Unlimited active accounts
