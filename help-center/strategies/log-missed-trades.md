# How to Log Missed Trades

## Overview

Logging missed trades helps you track opportunities you didn't capitalize on, providing insights into rule adherence and pattern recognition.

## Method 1: Manual Trade Logging

## Using the Manual Trade Page

Create missed trade records through the standard Manual Trade entry process:

- Navigate to the Manual Trade page
- Enter trade details as normal
- Apply the "missed" tag to the trade entry

## Visibility Across Dashboard

Trades logged via this method appear in multiple locations:

- Calendar - Shows missed trades in timeline view
- Reports - Included in overall performance analysis
- Dashboard - Visible in summary metrics and visualizations

## Method 2: Within Strategy

## Strategy-Specific Missed Trades Tab

Log missed trades directly within a specific strategy:

- Navigate to Strategies
- Select the relevant strategy
- Access the Missed Trades tab
- Record missed opportunities within that strategy context

## Limited Visibility

Missed trades logged via this method have restricted visibility:

- Appear only within the Strategy itself
- Do not appear in Dashboard
- Do not appear in Calendar
- Do not appear in Reports

## Choosing Your Logging Method

## When to Use Manual Trade Logging

Use Method 1 when you want missed trades visible across your entire TradeZella dashboard and reports for comprehensive performance analysis.

## When to Use Strategy-Specific Logging

Use Method 2 when tracking missed trades within a specific strategy context for focused strategy refinement without affecting overall dashboard metrics.
