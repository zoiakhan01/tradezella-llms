# How to Delete a Strategy

Over time, you may want to remove strategies that are no longer in use or relevant to your trading. Deleting a strategy from your TradeZella account is straightforward and can be done in a few simple steps.

## When to Delete a Strategy

You might consider deleting a strategy if:

- The strategy is no longer profitable or effective
- You've replaced it with a better approach
- You want to clean up your strategy library
- The strategy parameters are outdated
- You're consolidating multiple similar strategies

## How to Delete a Strategy

To permanently delete a strategy from your account:

1. Open TradeZella and navigate to the Strategies section
2. Locate the strategy you wish to delete
3. Click on the strategy to select it
4. Click the Menu button (three dots) or Delete button
5. Select "Delete Strategy" from the dropdown menu
6. Confirm the deletion when prompted

## Confirmation Dialog

Before permanently removing your strategy, TradeZella will display a confirmation dialog:

- Review the strategy name one final time
- The confirmation will state "Are you sure you want to permanently delete this strategy?"
- Click "Yes, Delete" to confirm or "Cancel" to abort

## Important Notes

- **Permanent Action**: Deleting a strategy cannot be undone. The strategy will be removed from your account permanently.
- **Trade History**: Your trade history associated with this strategy will remain intact and available in your reports.
- **Shared Links**: Any shareable links to this strategy will become inactive after deletion.
- **Backup**: Consider exporting or documenting your strategy parameters before deletion if you think you may need them later.

## Alternative: Archive Instead

If you're unsure about deletion, consider archiving your strategy instead:

- Archive strategies you're not actively using
- Keep them accessible without cluttering your active list
- Restore archived strategies at any time
