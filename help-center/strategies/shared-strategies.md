# What are Shared Strategies

## Overview

Shared Strategies allow you to access and learn from trading methodologies created by other TradeZella users, with flexible permissions around duplication and modification.

## Accessing Shared Strategies

## Shared with Me Tab

View all strategies that other traders have shared with you in the dedicated "Shared with me" tab within the Strategies section.

## Duplicating Shared Strategies

## Copying to Your Account

If the strategy creator has enabled duplication:

- Click the three-dot menu next to the shared strategy
- Select "Download" to copy the strategy to your account
- The copied strategy becomes your own, allowing customization

## Sharing Your Strategies

## Creating Sharing Links

Generate sharing links to distribute your strategies to other traders without requiring email invitations.

## Email Invitations

Share strategies directly with specific traders via email invitation.

## Controlling Duplication

## Permission Settings

When sharing a strategy, control whether recipients can:

- Enable duplication - Allow recipients to download and customize your strategy
- Disable duplication - Restrict recipients to viewing only

## Access Management

## Revoking Access

Revoke sharing access to your strategies at any time, preventing continued access by previous recipients.
