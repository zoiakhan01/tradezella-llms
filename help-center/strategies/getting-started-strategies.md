# Getting Started with Strategies

## Overview

Strategies in TradeZella enable you to formalize your trading approach, track rule adherence, and measure performance against defined criteria.

## Creating a New Strategy

## Strategy Basics

When creating a strategy, define the following elements:

- Name - A descriptive identifier for your strategy
- Description - Detailed explanation of the strategy concept and methodology
- Icon - Visual representation for quick identification
- Color - Distinctive color coding for easy visual reference

## Adding Custom Trading Rules

## Rule Definition

Define the specific conditions and patterns that trigger your trades:

- Entry criteria - Conditions required to enter a trade
- Exit criteria - Rules for closing positions
- Market conditions - Environmental factors affecting trade validity

## Learning from Templates

Access proven strategies created by professional traders:

- Kane strategies - Template strategies from trader Kane
- Jade Cap strategies - Template strategies from Jade Cap
- Community strategies - Additional shared trader methodologies

## Performance Metrics

## Key Strategy Indicators

Track important metrics for each strategy:

- Expectancy - Average profit per trade
- Win Rate - Percentage of profitable trades
- Profit Factor - Ratio of gains to losses

## Logging Missed Trades

Record trading opportunities you missed, allowing the system to track adherence to your strategy rules and identify patterns in missed setups.

## Community Features

## Sharing and Collaboration

Share your strategies with the TradeZella community to receive feedback, and access strategies shared by other traders.
