# How to Share Strategies

Sharing your trading strategies with other TradeZella users is a powerful way to collaborate, teach, and build a community of traders. TradeZella makes it easy to generate shareable links for your custom strategies.

## Why Share Strategies?

Sharing your strategies allows you to:
- Collaborate with other traders
- Get feedback on your trading approach
- Help mentees learn your methods
- Build credibility within the trading community
- Document your trading methodology

## How to Generate a Shareable Link

To share a strategy with other users, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to the Strategies section in your TradeZella dashboard
2. Select the strategy you want to share
3. Click the Share button or Share icon
4. Choose "Generate Shareable Link"
5. Copy the unique link provided
6. Share the link with other TradeZella users via email, messaging, or any communication platform

## Strategy Sharing Options

TradeZella offers flexible sharing options:

- **Public Link**: Create a publicly accessible link anyone can view
- **View Only**: Recipients can see your strategy details but cannot modify them
- **Request Access**: Allow recipients to request permission before viewing

## Viewing Shared Strategies

When you receive a shareable link to a strategy:

1. Click the link in your browser
2. Review the strategy details and rules
3. Clone the strategy to your account (if permitted)
4. Customize it for your own trading

## Managing Shared Links

You can manage your strategy shares:

- Revoke access to any shared link at any time
- Track who has accessed your shared strategies
- View analytics on strategy views and clones
- Update sharing permissions

## Privacy and Security

Your personal account data remains private. When sharing a strategy, only the strategy rules and configuration are shared, not your trade history or account balance.
