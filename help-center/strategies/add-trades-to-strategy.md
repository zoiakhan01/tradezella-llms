# How to Add Trades to Your Strategy

Once you've created a strategy in TradeZella, you'll want to assign your trades to it for tracking and analysis. There are two primary methods to add trades to your strategy: manual assignment and automatic tagging rules.

## Manual Trade Assignment

Manual assignment is useful when you want to specifically categorize individual trades:

1. Navigate to your Trades section
2. Find the trade you want to assign to a strategy
3. Click on the trade to open its details
4. Look for the "Strategy" field or dropdown
5. Select the desired strategy from the list
6. Save the changes

You can manually assign trades one at a time or use bulk actions to assign multiple trades to a strategy at once.

## Bulk Assignment

To assign multiple trades to a strategy efficiently:

1. Go to your Trades section
2. Use the filter or search to locate relevant trades
3. Select multiple trades using the checkboxes
4. Click "Bulk Actions" or "Edit Selected"
5. Choose "Assign to Strategy"
6. Select the target strategy
7. Confirm the bulk assignment

## Automatic Tagging Rules

Set up rules to automatically assign trades to strategies based on criteria:

1. Navigate to Settings or Strategy Rules
2. Select "Auto-Tag Rules" or "Automatic Assignment"
3. Create a new rule with conditions such as:
   - Entry time (specific hours or sessions)
   - Instrument (specific symbols or pairs)
   - Entry price range or zones
   - Trade duration or holding time
   - Profit/loss thresholds
4. Assign the rule to your strategy
5. Enable the rule to start automatic categorization

## Rule Examples

Common auto-tagging rules include:

- Trades entered during London session → "London Session Strategy"
- Trades on EUR/USD with risk below 20 pips → "Conservative EUR/USD"
- Trades with profit target above 100 pips → "Swing Trading"
- Trades entered at specific support/resistance zones → "Zone Breakout"

## Strategy Filtering and Analysis

Once trades are assigned to strategies:

1. View strategy-specific performance metrics
2. Filter trades by strategy name
3. Analyze strategy win rate and profitability
4. Compare strategy performance over time
5. Generate strategy-focused reports

## Editing or Removing Strategy Assignments

To change a trade's strategy assignment:

1. Open the specific trade
2. Click on the Strategy field
3. Select a different strategy or "Remove Strategy"
4. Save your changes
