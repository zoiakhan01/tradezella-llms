# How the Dashboard Calendar Calculates P&L

The Dashboard Calendar provides a visual representation of your daily trading performance. Understanding how P&L is calculated helps you accurately interpret your trading results.

## P&L Calculation Basis

The calendar shows **daily P&L based on closed trades only**. Any open positions or unrealized gains/losses are not included in the calendar display. Only trades that are completely closed contribute to the daily P&L shown in the calendar.

## Net Realized P&L

The daily P&L displayed represents the **net realized P&L from all closed trades on that day**. This includes:
- All trades that closed on that specific date
- The complete profit or loss from each closed trade
- The net result after combining all closed trades' outcomes

## Commissions and Fees

**Commissions and fees are included** in the P&L calculation. Your displayed P&L represents your actual profit or loss after accounting for all trading costs, providing an accurate picture of your real trading results.

## Color Coding

The calendar uses consistent color coding to quickly identify daily performance:

- **Green** - Days with positive P&L (profits)
- **Red** - Days with negative P&L (losses)
- **Grey** - Days with breakeven results or no trading activity

This visual system makes it easy to scan your trading history and identify your best and worst trading days at a glance.

## Dashboard View Considerations

The calendar respects your current **dashboard view setting**, which affects how P&L is displayed:

### View Examples
- **Dollars View** - Shows P&L in absolute currency (e.g., +$500, -$250)
- **Percentage View** - Shows P&L as a percentage of account balance
- **R-Multiple View** - Shows P&L in terms of risk units
- **Privacy View** - Shows colored indicators without specific amounts
- **Ticks/Pips/Points** - Shows P&L in market-specific units

Switching your dashboard view instantly updates how the calendar displays the same underlying data.

## Calendar Variants

TradeZella provides four different calendar variants for different analysis needs:

### Mini Calendar
A compact monthly view ideal for quick overview or when dashboard space is limited.

### Standard Calendar
The traditional calendar format showing daily P&L in a standard monthly layout.

### Advanced Calendar
Enhanced calendar with monthly totals, weekly breakdowns, multiple stat views per day, and journal entry indicators.

### Yearly Calendar
A full-year view allowing you to see your trading performance across the entire year at once.

## What's Not Included

The calendar does not display:
- Unrealized P&L from open positions
- Pending orders or trades awaiting execution
- Funding deposits or withdrawals
- Account adjustments or corrections
- Commissions as separate line items (they're included in the net P&L)
