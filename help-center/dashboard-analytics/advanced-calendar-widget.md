# Advanced Calendar Widget in TradeZella Dashboard

The Advanced Calendar widget provides a comprehensive view of your trading performance with detailed daily, weekly, and monthly insights.

## Layout and Structure

### Monthly Summary
At the top of the Advanced Calendar, view your **total P&L for the entire month** at a glance. This quick metric helps you assess overall monthly performance.

### Weekly Performance Breakdown
Below the monthly total, each week displays its own performance summary, allowing you to identify strong and weak trading weeks within the month.

### Daily Statistics
Each day in the calendar shows customizable statistics that can include:
- **P&L** - Daily profit or loss
- **Trade Count** - Number of trades executed on that day
- **Win Rate** - Percentage of winning trades for that day

## Journal Entry Indicators

Days that contain journal entries are specially marked with indicators, helping you correlate your trading notes with performance data. This makes it easy to reference reasons for specific trading outcomes.

## Color Coding System

The Advanced Calendar uses intuitive color coding to quickly identify trading results:

- **Green** - Days with profit/positive performance
- **Red** - Days with losses/negative performance
- **Grey** - Days with breakeven or no trading activity

This visual system allows you to quickly scan your trading history and identify patterns.

## Comparison with Standard Calendar Widget

### Standard Calendar Widget
- Shows daily P&L in a traditional month view
- Basic color coding (green/red/grey)
- Minimal additional information per day

### Advanced Calendar Widget - Enhanced Features
The Advanced Calendar builds on the standard version with:
- **Monthly total P&L** displayed at the top
- **Weekly performance breakdown** for each week
- **Multiple stat views** per day (selectable to show P&L, trade count, win rate, or other metrics)
- **Journal entry indicators** to mark days with trading notes
- More granular performance analysis within a single view

## Using the Advanced Calendar

The Advanced Calendar is ideal for:
- Identifying your strongest and weakest trading weeks
- Correlating daily performance with journal entries
- Analyzing multiple metrics (P&L, win rate, trade frequency) simultaneously
- Reviewing monthly performance with weekly context
- Spotting patterns in your trading behavior across different timeframes
