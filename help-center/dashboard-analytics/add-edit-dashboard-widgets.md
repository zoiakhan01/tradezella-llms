# How to Add / Edit Dashboard Widgets

Customize your TradeZella dashboard to display the metrics most relevant to your trading strategy. The dashboard is divided into two editable sections with different capabilities.

## Editing Upper Section Widgets

The upper section displays up to 5 key widgets for quick performance overview.

### To Add or Remove Upper Section Widgets

1. Click the **pencil icon** in the upper section header
2. Select which widgets to display (maximum of 5)
3. Add or remove widgets based on your preferences
4. Click **Save** to apply your changes

The upper section updates immediately with your new widget configuration.

## Editing Lower Section Widgets

The lower section provides space for detailed analysis widgets and can accommodate more content.

### To Add, Arrange, or Remove Lower Section Widgets

1. Click the **Template** button
2. Select **Edit** from the dropdown menu
3. Click the **"+"** button to add new widgets
4. **Drag** widgets to rearrange their order and position
5. Click the **"x"** button on any widget to remove it
6. Click **Save** to confirm your changes

## Saving and Managing Templates

Templates allow you to save multiple dashboard layouts for different trading scenarios or analysis focuses.

### Creating Templates

Once you've arranged your lower section widgets to your preference:
1. Complete your widget arrangement in edit mode
2. Click **Save** - you can save this configuration as a new template
3. Give your template a descriptive name

### Switching Between Templates

- Click the **Template** button anytime to view all saved layouts
- Select any template to instantly switch to that dashboard configuration
- Switch between templates at any time without losing your saved configurations

## Best Practices

- Create templates for different trading styles or timeframes
- Save a "quick overview" template with essential metrics
- Save a "detailed analysis" template with comprehensive data
- Use templates to optimize dashboard performance on different devices
