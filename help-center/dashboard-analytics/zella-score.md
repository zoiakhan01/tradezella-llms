# Introducing the All-New Zella Score

The Zella Score is a comprehensive, single-number metric designed to evaluate your overall trading performance by combining six key dimensions of trading success into a 0-100 scale.

## What Is the Zella Score?

The Zella Score provides a holistic assessment of your trading performance by weighing multiple important metrics. Rather than tracking dozens of statistics, this composite score gives you one actionable number to understand where you stand as a trader.

## Score Range

The Zella Score ranges from **0 to 100**, with higher scores indicating stronger overall trading performance across all measured dimensions.

## The Six Metrics

The Zella Score combines six critical trading metrics, each weighted according to their importance:

### Avg Win/Loss Ratio (20% weight)
Measures the average size of your winning trades relative to your losing trades. A higher ratio indicates better risk-reward management. This 20% weighting reflects the importance of favorable win/loss mechanics in sustainable trading.

### Trade Win % (15% weight)
Represents the percentage of your trades that are profitable. While important, this is weighted at 15% because high win rates alone don't guarantee profitability without proper position sizing. A trader with 40% win rate but excellent risk-reward can outperform a trader with 60% win rate and poor risk-reward.

### Max Drawdown (20% weight)
Tracks the largest peak-to-trough decline in your account. This 20% weighting reflects how critical risk management is to long-term success. Controlling drawdown is essential for preserving capital and maintaining psychological resilience.

### Profit Factor (25% weight)
Calculated as gross profit divided by gross loss, this receives the highest weighting at 25%. A profit factor above 1.5 is considered strong, as it indicates gross profits significantly exceed gross losses.

### Recovery Factor (10% weight)
Measures how quickly you recover from drawdowns. This 10% weight acknowledges that while drawdown is important, your ability to bounce back is equally critical for maintaining a healthy trading equity curve.

### Consistency Score (10% weight)
Evaluates the consistency of your trading results across your trading period. This metric identifies whether your performance is steady or erratic, with consistent performance being more predictable and sustainable.

## Adding Zella Score to Your Dashboard

The Zella Score is available as a dashboard widget that you can add to either your upper or lower section:

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click the pencil icon (upper section) or Template → Edit (lower section)
3. Select **Zella Score** from available widgets
4. Save your changes

The Zella Score widget will now display on your dashboard, updating automatically as your trading performance changes.

## Using Zella Score for Improvement

The Zella Score identifies your strongest and weakest areas:

- If your score is low, review which metrics are pulling it down (indicated by the component breakdowns)
- Focus improvement efforts on the highest-weighted metrics (Profit Factor and Max Drawdown)
- Track how changes to your strategy affect your overall score
- Use it as a single metric to benchmark progress over time

## What Makes It Actionable

Unlike vanity metrics that only celebrate wins, the Zella Score provides:
- **Balanced perspective** - Combines profitability, risk management, and consistency
- **Clear priorities** - Weightings show which areas matter most for success
- **Improvement direction** - Identifies specific metrics to target for score improvement
- **Long-term focus** - Emphasizes sustainable, repeatable trading performance
