# Dashboard Percentage View in TradeZella

The Percentage View displays all dashboard metrics as percentages instead of absolute dollar amounts, making it easier to compare performance across accounts of different sizes.

## What Is the Percentage View?

Instead of showing your P&L, drawdown, and other metrics in dollars, the Percentage View expresses them as percentages relative to your account balance. This normalization allows you to assess trading performance independently of account size.

## When to Use Percentage View

The Percentage View is particularly valuable in these scenarios:

### Comparing Across Accounts
If you trade multiple accounts with different sizes, percentage view lets you compare their performance fairly. A $1,000 profit on a $10,000 account (10%) differs significantly from $1,000 profit on a $100,000 account (1%).

### Evaluating Consistency
Track whether your returns are consistent relative to your capital, not just in absolute terms. This helps identify whether you're truly improving your trading or just risking more money.

### Risk Assessment
Assess your risk-taking relative to your account size. Drawdown expressed as a percentage of equity tells you whether your account volatility is consistent across different account sizes.

### Strategy Backtesting
Compare a strategy tested on different account sizes by normalizing results to percentages.

## Percentage Calculations

All dashboard metrics are recalculated as percentages based on your account balance when in Percentage View:

- **P&L %** = (Profit or Loss / Account Balance) × 100
- **Drawdown %** = (Drawdown Amount / Peak Account Balance) × 100
- **Win/Loss Amounts %** = (Average Win or Loss / Account Balance) × 100
- Other metrics are similarly normalized to percentage equivalents

## Accessing Percentage View

Switching to Percentage View takes just two steps:

1. Click the **View** button in the top bar of your dashboard
2. Select **Percentage** from the dropdown menu

Your entire dashboard instantly updates to show all metrics in percentage format.

## What Changes in Percentage View

When you switch to Percentage View:
- All P&L values display as percentages
- Drawdown metrics show as percentages of peak equity
- Win/loss amounts appear as percentages of account balance
- Calendar daily P&L shows as percentage gains/losses
- All other metrics adjust to percentage equivalents

## What Stays the Same

Some metrics may display identically in both views:
- Win % (already a percentage) remains unchanged
- Ratio-based metrics (Profit Factor, Win/Loss Ratio) don't change
- Count-based metrics (Total Trades, Trade Count) remain the same

## Combining with Other Views

You can also combine Percentage View with other specialized views. For example, if you trade futures:
- Select Percentage View to see returns as percentages
- Combined with Ticks or Points view to measure in contract-specific units

## Benefits of Percentage Normalization

- **Fair Comparison** - Compare performance across different account sizes
- **Growth Tracking** - Measure whether you're consistently growing your capital
- **Risk Awareness** - Understand actual risk relative to your capital
- **Scalability Assessment** - See if your strategy scales proportionally
- **Psychological Perspective** - Focus on returns relative to equity, not absolute numbers
