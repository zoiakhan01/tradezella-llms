# Understanding Dashboard Widgets and Stats

The TradeZella dashboard provides comprehensive trading statistics and performance metrics through various widgets. Understanding each widget will help you monitor your trading performance effectively.

## Upper Section Widgets

The upper section of your dashboard displays up to 5 key performance indicators at a glance:

### Core Metrics
- **Net P&L** - Your total profit or loss across all trades
- **Win %** - The percentage of winning trades out of total trades
- **Profit Factor** - Ratio of gross profit to gross loss
- **Average Win/Loss** - Mean profit on winning trades versus mean loss on losing trades
- **Total Trades** - Complete count of all trades executed

### Advanced Metrics
- **Best/Worst Trade** - Your highest profit and largest loss in a single trade
- **Consecutive Wins/Losses** - Current or historical winning/losing streaks
- **Max Drawdown** - The largest peak-to-trough decline in your account equity
- **Average Drawdown** - Mean drawdown magnitude across your trading period

## Lower Section Widgets

The lower section contains 17+ specialized widgets for deeper analysis:

### Performance Visualization
- **Calendar** - Daily P&L overview in a monthly calendar format
- **Advanced Calendar** - Enhanced calendar with weekly breakdowns and multiple stat views per day
- **Equity Curve** - Visual representation of account growth over time
- **Cumulative P&L** - Running total of profits and losses
- **Daily P&L** - Isolated daily performance metrics

### Risk and Distribution Analysis
- **Distribution** - Histogram showing frequency of P&L outcomes
- **Drawdown** - Drawdown tracking across your trading history
- **Win vs Loss** - Visual comparison of winning versus losing trades

### Trader Metrics and Tools
- **Zella Score** - Composite performance score combining multiple metrics
- **Progress Tracker** - Monitor progress toward trading goals
- **Trades Table** - Detailed listing of all individual trades with metrics

## Dashboard Views

Switch between seven different view modes to analyze your data from different perspectives:

### Available Views
- **Dollars** - Displays all metrics in absolute currency values (default view)
- **Percentage** - Shows all metrics as percentages relative to account balance
- **R-Multiple** - Expresses results in terms of risk units per trade
- **Privacy** - Obscures sensitive account values for screenshots and sharing
- **Ticks** - Specialized view for futures traders showing movement in ticks
- **Pips** - Forex-specific view displaying results in pips
- **Points** - Futures-focused view using point measurements
