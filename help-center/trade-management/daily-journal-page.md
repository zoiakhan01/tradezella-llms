# Understanding the Daily Journal Page

The Daily Trade Insights page breaks down your trading performance for each trading day, providing a comprehensive view of your daily metrics and trade details.

## Key Features

### Mini Calendar View
The page displays a mini calendar showing your trading days color-coded by performance:
- **Green days** indicate days with profits
- **Red days** indicate days with losses

### Expanding Trading Days
Expand any trading day to see all trades executed in chronological order. This helps you review the sequence of your trading activity and understand how your day unfolded.

### Intraday Cumulative Net P&L Chart
Visualize how your realized P&L fluctuated throughout the trading day as trades closed. This chart provides insights into your intraday performance dynamics.

## Daily Statistics

Each trading day displays comprehensive performance metrics:
- Net P&L
- Total Trades
- Win Rate
- Winners (number of profitable trades)
- Losers (number of losing trades)
- Volume
- Profit Factor
- Commissions
- Gross P&L

## Additional Capabilities

### Trading Journal
Write notes and journal entries directly on your trading days to document lessons learned, trading thoughts, and observations about your performance.

### Zella Insights
Access AI-powered insights related to your daily trading performance.

### Customizable Trade Detail Columns
Customize which columns appear in your trade details to focus on the metrics most important to your trading strategy.
