# How to Save Chart Indicators and Settings as Default

Customizing your trading charts with technical indicators, drawing tools, and display settings is essential for analyzing your trades effectively. TradeZella allows you to save your preferred chart configuration as default settings, automatically applying them to every trade you review in the future. This saves time and ensures consistency in how you analyze your trades.

Rather than manually adding the same indicators and adjusting chart settings for each trade, you can configure your ideal trading setup once and have it persist across all trades. This feature streamlines your workflow and helps you maintain a consistent analytical approach to trade review and performance analysis.

Whether you use moving averages, RSI, volume profiles, trend lines, or other technical tools, saving them as defaults ensures you see the same analytical view for every trade in your journal.

## Key Features/Steps

### Add Indicators to Your Chart

Open any trade from your Trade Log to view its Trade Page. On the interactive chart, look for the indicators or tools menu, typically represented by a "+" icon or labeled "Add Indicator." Click this button to see the available indicators that TradeZella supports, which typically include moving averages, RSI, Bollinger Bands, MACD, Stochastic, volume profiles, and many others.

Select the indicators you want to use for analyzing your trades. As you add each indicator, you'll see it plotted on the chart. You can add multiple indicators simultaneously. Each indicator can typically be customized with parameters such as period length for moving averages or overbought/oversold levels for oscillators.

### Customize Indicator Parameters

For each indicator you add, customize its parameters to match your trading strategy. For moving averages, set the period (such as 20, 50, or 200 periods). For RSI or other oscillators, adjust sensitivity settings. You can modify line colors, styles, and placement on the chart. Take time to get the visualization exactly as you prefer it, as these settings will become your default for all future trade reviews.

### Configure Chart Display Settings

Beyond indicators, configure general chart settings such as candlestick colors (green for up, red for down), chart background color, grid display, and time zone. Adjust the default timeframe display and candle size. Enable or disable volume bars if desired. These visual settings contribute to how clearly you can analyze each trade's price action.

### Draw Annotations

Use TradeZella's drawing tools to add trend lines, support and resistance levels, or other annotations to your chart. These tools help you mark significant price levels or chart patterns. While annotations made on individual trades are trade-specific, your preference for which drawing tools are available will be reflected in default settings.

### Save as Default Settings

Once you've configured your indicators, customized all parameters, and set your preferred display options, look for a "Save as Default" or "Save Settings" button. This is typically located in the chart settings menu or toolbar. Click this button to save your entire chart configuration as the default for all future trades you review.

A confirmation message will appear indicating that your settings have been saved. From this point forward, whenever you open a trade from your Trade Log, the chart will automatically load with all your saved indicators, custom parameters, and display preferences applied.

### Applying Defaults to Existing Trades

Your saved default settings apply to new trades you review going forward. If you want to apply these same defaults to previously reviewed trades, you may need to use an "Apply Defaults" option or manually load them in each trade. Check your TradeZella version's specific options for bulk application of settings.

### Modifying Default Settings

You can change your default chart settings at any time by adjusting the indicators and settings on any trade, then selecting "Update Defaults" instead of just "Save Settings." This overwrites your previous defaults with your new configuration.
