# How to Log Daily Notes on the Daily Journal Page

Recording daily notes and journal entries helps you document lessons learned and reflect on your trading performance throughout the day.

## Adding Notes to Trading Days

### For Days with Trades
Click the "Write a Note" icon on the trading day to add notes and journal entries. This allows you to document observations and lessons learned from your trading activity.

### For Days Without Trades
Click "Log Day" to add notes even on days when you didn't execute any trades. This is useful for recording market observations or trading plan adjustments.

## Note Entry Process

A pop-up window appears that allows you to:
- Document lessons learned from the trading day
- Record trading observations and insights
- Note adjustments to your trading strategy
- Capture any important market conditions or events

## Saving Notes

Notes must be saved to attach them to the trading day. Once saved, your notes will be permanently associated with that specific trading day in your journal.
