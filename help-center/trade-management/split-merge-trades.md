# How Can I Split or Merge a Trade

TradeZella automatically groups executions into logical trades for you. However, you can manually split or merge trades when you need to reorganize your trading data.

## Understanding Trade Grouping

TradeZella automatically groups multiple executions into logical trades based on symbols, accounts, and other relevant criteria. This helps organize your trading data in a meaningful way.

## How to Merge Trades

Merging combines multiple trades into a single logical trade.

### Steps to Merge
1. **Check trade boxes** - Select the trades you want to merge
2. **Access Bulk Actions** - Click the Bulk Actions menu
3. **Select Merge Trades** - Choose the "Merge trades" option
4. **Choose executions** - Select which executions to include in the merged trade
5. **Confirm** - Finalize the merge operation

## How to Split a Trade

Splitting separates a grouped trade into individual or smaller logical trades.

### Steps to Split
1. **Select a trade** - Click on the trade you want to split
2. **Access Bulk Actions** - Click the Bulk Actions menu
3. **Select Split Trade** - Choose the "Split trade" option
4. **Choose executions** - Select which executions to separate from the trade
5. **Click SPLIT** - Finalize the split operation

## Important Considerations

### Merge Restrictions
Only merge trades that share the same characteristics:
- **Same ticker/symbol** - Trades must be in the same security
- **Same account** - All trades must be from the same trading account
- **Same contract type** - Futures, options, stocks, etc. must be consistent
- **Same instrument** - The underlying instrument must match

Do not attempt to merge trades with different tickers, accounts, contract types, or instruments.
