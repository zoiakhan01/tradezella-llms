# How to Transfer Trades Between Accounts

TradeZella allows you to organize your trades across multiple trading accounts within a single platform. Whether you're consolidating trades or reorganizing your account structure, the ability to transfer trades between accounts provides flexibility in managing your trading journal. This feature is particularly useful when you've entered trades in the wrong account or need to reorganize your trading data.

Transferring trades is a straightforward process that maintains all the original trade data including entry and exit prices, dates, notes, screenshots, and any custom tags or annotations you've added. The transfer preserves the complete history and context of each trade while moving it to the destination account you specify.

Understanding how to use this feature effectively helps you maintain accurate records across multiple trading accounts and ensures your performance metrics are calculated correctly for each account individually.

## Key Features/Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to Trade Log

Access your Trade Log page from the main navigation menu. This view displays all your trades in a comprehensive list format with columns for entry price, exit price, date, profit/loss, and other relevant metrics. The Trade Log gives you a complete overview of your trading activity and is the primary location for performing bulk trade operations.

### Step 2: Select Trades for Transfer

In the Trade Log, locate the trades you wish to transfer to another account. Use the checkbox next to each trade to select one or multiple trades. You can select individual trades by clicking their checkboxes, or use the "Select All" option at the top of the list to select all visible trades. The interface will show you a count of how many trades are currently selected.

### Step 3: Choose Destination Account

After selecting your trades, look for the transfer action button in the toolbar or right-click menu. Select the transfer option and a dialog will appear showing a list of your available trading accounts. Choose the destination account where you want to move the selected trades. Make sure you're selecting the correct account, as the transfer will move all selected trades to that location.

### Step 4: Confirm Transfer

Review the summary showing which trades will be transferred and to which account. The confirmation dialog will display the number of trades being transferred and the destination account name. Click "Confirm Transfer" to complete the operation. TradeZella will process the transfer and update your Trade Log immediately.

### After Transfer

Once the transfer is complete, the trades will appear in the destination account's Trade Log and will be included in that account's performance calculations. The original account will no longer contain those trades. All trade details including execution prices, dates, and notes remain intact during the transfer process.
