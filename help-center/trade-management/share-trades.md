# How to Share Your Trades from TradeZella

Sharing your trades with other traders, mentors, or on social media is an effective way to get feedback, build your trading community, or document your trading journey. TradeZella makes it simple to generate shareable links for individual trades that display the complete trade information including the price chart, entry and exit markers, statistics, and your trade notes.

When you share a trade via TradeZella's sharing feature, recipients see a professional presentation of your trade with all relevant context. The shared view includes your chart with indicators, the entry and exit prices marked clearly, and your analysis notes, giving viewers a complete picture of your trade rationale and execution.

Sharing trades is a powerful tool for networking in the trading community, documenting your best setups, or getting constructive feedback on your trading decisions.

## Key Features/Steps

### Generate a Share Link

Open the trade you want to share from your Trade Log by clicking on it to access the Trade Page. On the Trade Page, look for a "Share" button or icon, typically located in the top-right area of the page. Click this button to open the sharing options panel. TradeZella will generate a unique shareable link for this specific trade.

The share link is a direct URL that anyone can access without needing a TradeZella account. The link is permanent for your trade and won't change unless you modify your sharing preferences. You can generate the share link once and use it multiple times to share across different platforms.

### Customize Privacy Settings

Before sharing, review the privacy settings for the shared trade. Determine what information you want to be visible to recipients. Most TradeZella sharing options allow you to control whether viewers can see your account balance, trading account name, full notes, or other sensitive details. Configure these settings based on your comfort level with privacy.

Some traders prefer to hide their account size while still showing the trade setup and decision-making process. Others are comfortable sharing complete information. Customize the settings to match your preferences for each shared trade.

### Copy and Share the Link

Once your share link is generated and privacy settings are configured, copy the link from the sharing panel. You can now paste this link in various locations: email it directly to other traders, post it on trading forums or discussion boards, share it on social media platforms like Twitter or LinkedIn, or include it in blog posts about your trading journey.

When recipients click the link, they'll see a professional view of your trade with the chart, your entry and exit points clearly marked, key statistics, and any notes you've added. They can zoom and pan the chart to examine your setup in detail.

### What Recipients See

People who receive your shared trade link will see the trade chart with candlestick price action and your marked entry and exit points. They'll view key metrics like profit/loss percentage, hold duration, and risk-to-reward ratio. Your trade notes and analysis will be visible, giving them insight into your trading thought process. They won't be able to modify the trade or access other trades in your journal unless you specifically grant additional permissions.

### Managing Shared Trades

You can view a list of all your shared trades and their sharing status from your account settings or profile page. From this management interface, you can disable sharing for any trade at any time, revoking access to the share link. You can also re-enable sharing if you previously disabled it.

### Using Shared Trades for Collaboration

Sharing trades is particularly valuable when seeking mentorship or feedback. Share a trade with a trading mentor who can provide comments and suggestions. Share educational setups with other traders you're mentoring. Use trade sharing as part of your content creation if you publish trading analysis or educational material.
