# How Can I Delete a Trade

Deleting trades allows you to remove incorrect entries or unwanted data from your trading journal. TradeZella provides multiple ways to delete trades depending on where you're viewing them. Once deleted, trades are permanently removed from your account and cannot be recovered, so make sure you're deleting the correct trade before confirming the action.

TradeZella requires explicit confirmation before deleting any trade to prevent accidental data loss. This safety feature ensures you have a moment to review the trade details before permanent removal. Whether you're cleaning up your Trade Log, managing individual trades, or reviewing your Daily Journal, the deletion process is straightforward and user-friendly.

Understanding the different deletion methods available helps you manage your trading data efficiently. Each deletion method is accessible from different parts of the application, giving you flexibility in how you organize and maintain your trading records.

## Key Features/Steps

### Deleting from the Trade Log Page

Navigate to the Trade Log page in your dashboard. Browse through your list of trades and locate the trade you wish to delete. Click the three-dot menu icon (⋯) next to the trade entry to reveal the action menu. Select the "Delete" option from the dropdown menu. A confirmation dialog will appear asking you to verify the deletion. Review the trade details to ensure you're deleting the correct entry, then click "Confirm Delete" to permanently remove the trade from your account.

### Deleting from Individual Trade Page

Open the specific trade you want to delete by clicking on its entry in the Trade Log. On the Trade Page, you'll see detailed information about the trade including entry price, exit price, profit/loss, and notes. Look for the delete button, typically located in the top-right corner or in the options menu. Click the delete button and confirm your action in the popup dialog. The trade will be permanently removed from your journal.

### Deleting from Daily Journal

Access your Daily Journal view where trades are grouped by the date they were executed. Find the trade you wish to delete within a specific date entry. Hover over or click on the trade row to reveal the action buttons. Select the delete option and confirm the removal when prompted. The trade will be removed from both your daily view and overall Trade Log.

### Important Considerations

Before deleting trades, consider that deletion is permanent and irreversible. If you're unsure about a trade but want to keep the data, you might want to consider adding a note or tag instead. However, if you need to remove the trade completely, ensure you've double-checked the trade details in the confirmation dialog. Some users prefer to export their trades to CSV before making bulk deletions as a backup.
