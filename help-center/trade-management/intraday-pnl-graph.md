# Understanding the Daily Journal Intraday Cumulative Net P&L Graph

The Intraday Cumulative Net P&L Graph shows how your realized P&L fluctuated throughout the trading day as you closed trades. This visualization helps you understand your intraday performance dynamics and identify key moments in your trading session.

## What the Graph Reveals

The chart answers critical questions about your daily trading performance:

### Performance Questions
- **Did I leave profits on the table?** Review peak P&L points and compare them to closing performance to identify missed opportunities.
- **What was the highest amount I was up?** Track your maximum intraday equity to understand your best case scenario for the day.
- **How did P&L change throughout the day?** Observe the trajectory of your cumulative P&L to identify patterns in your trading session progression.
- **Which trade cost the most?** Identify the largest drawdown or worst-performing trade during the session.

## Using the Graph

### Full Screen Expansion
Expand the chart to full screen for a more detailed view of your intraday P&L movements. This allows for better visibility of price movements and trade execution points.

### Interactive Hover Feature
Hover over specific points on the chart to see the corresponding trades. This helps you connect P&L changes to specific trades and understand the impact of each execution on your overall performance.
