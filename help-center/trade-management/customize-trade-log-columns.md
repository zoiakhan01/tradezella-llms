# How to Customize the Trade Log Columns

Customizing your Trade Log columns allows you to focus on the metrics that matter most to your trading strategy and analysis approach.

## Steps to Customize Columns

### Access Column Settings
Navigate to the Trade Log page and locate the three-dot button in the upper right corner of the interface.

### Select/Deselect Columns
Click the three-dot button to open the column customization menu. From here, you can:
- **Select columns** to display metrics you want to see
- **Deselect columns** to hide metrics you don't need

## Benefits of Customization

### Strategy-Specific Analysis
Choose columns that align with your specific trading strategy. For example, if you focus on momentum trading, you might prioritize entry price and exit price columns.

### Pattern Recognition
Customizing your view helps you spot patterns in your trading behavior and identify correlations between specific variables and your trading performance.

### Statistical Analysis
Display columns that allow you to analyze statistics relevant to your trading system, such as duration, profit factor, win rate, and other performance indicators.
