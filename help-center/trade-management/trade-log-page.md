# Understanding the Trade Log Page

The Trade Log Page is your central command center for managing all trading activity. It provides a comprehensive overview of your trades with powerful tools for analysis and organization.

## Key Performance Widgets

The Trade Log Page displays four essential performance metrics:

### Net Cumulative P&L
Your total profit or loss across all trades displayed in the trade log view.

### Profit Factor
The ratio of gross profit to gross loss, indicating the overall profitability of your trading system.

### Trade Win %
The percentage of trades that resulted in a profit, also known as your win rate.

### Average Win/Loss per Trade
The average profit on winning trades and average loss on losing trades, providing insight into your risk-reward profile.

## Trade Management Features

### Viewing and Organizing
- **Customize columns** to display the metrics most relevant to your analysis
- **Sort trades** by any column to organize your data
- **Filter by various criteria** to focus on specific subsets of trades (date range, symbol, strategy, etc.)

### Trade Modification
- **Merge trades** to group related executions
- **Split trades** to separate grouped executions
- **Tag trades** for better organization and categorization

### Data Management
- **Export to CSV** for external analysis and record-keeping
- **Delete trades** when necessary
- **Transfer trades between accounts** to reorganize your trading data

### Bulk Actions
Perform operations on multiple trades simultaneously for efficient management of your trading activity.
