# Prop Firm Sync

Prop Firm Sync is a unified dashboard inside TradeZella for tracking all your prop firm finances, accounts, and performance in one place.(coming soon)

## The Problem

Prop firm traders often juggle multiple accounts across different firms with no clear picture of their total spending, ROI, or which firms and challenge types work best for their trading style. Spreadsheets get messy fast.

## What Prop Firm Sync Does

### Finance Tracking
- Connect your bank via **Plaid** for automatic transaction detection — evaluation purchases, resets, activation fees, and payouts are identified automatically
- Or track manually by creating accounts and logging expenses
- View total spending, spending by firm, spending by challenge type and size
- See your **net total and true ROI** across all prop firm activity

### Account Monitoring
- Create and track individual challenge accounts
- Connect challenges to your TradeZella trading accounts
- Monitor account balance, daily loss limits, max drawdown rules, and profit targets
- Track account status: active, passed, failed, funded

### Performance Analytics
- **Pass rate by firm** — Which firms do you pass most often?
- **Pass rate by account type** — Which challenge sizes and configurations work best?
- **Failure reason analysis** — Are you failing from daily loss limits, max drawdown, consistency rules, or other reasons?
- **Best performing firm and challenge type** — Data-driven recommendations
- **Fastest to payout** — Track time to funded status and first payout
- Every failed account links back to the trades that caused the failure

### Challenge Simulator (Beta)
- Simulate your trading performance against different challenge rules **before spending money**
- Uses your actual trade history to project outcomes
- Match your trading style to challenge configurations
- Eliminate wasted evaluations by identifying challenges you're unlikely to pass

## Supported Prop Firms

Prop Firm Sync supports tracking for **200+ prop firms** including:
- FTMO, MyFundedFX, Topstep, Apex Trader Funding, The Funded Trader, True Forex Funds, Funded Next, SurgeTrader, and many more

## How It Works

1. Navigate to **Prop Firm Sync** from the TradeZella menu
2. Connect your bank via Plaid (or choose manual tracking)
3. Create challenge accounts and link them to your trading accounts
4. TradeZella automatically tracks spending, performance, and outcomes
5. Review analytics to optimize your prop firm strategy

## Key Benefits

- **See your true ROI** — Know exactly how much you've spent vs earned across all firms
- **Eliminate guesswork** — Data shows which firms and challenges work for you
- **Learn from failures** — Every failed challenge links to the specific trades that caused it
- **Save money** — The Challenge Simulator helps you avoid challenges you're unlikely to pass
- **One dashboard** — No more messy spreadsheets tracking 10+ accounts across 5+ firms
