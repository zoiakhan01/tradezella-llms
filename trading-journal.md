# TradeZella Trading Journal & Analytics

The TradeZella Trading Journal is an automated trading journal and analytics platform. It helps traders log every trade, analyze their performance with 50+ reports, and improve through data-driven insights.

## Automatic Trade Importing

TradeZella supports three methods of getting trades into your journal:

1. **Auto-Sync** — Directly connect your broker account for automatic, real-time trade importing. Supported auto-sync brokers include MetaTrader 4/5, cTrader, Tradovate, and others
2. **File Upload** — Upload trade history CSV/Excel files exported from your broker. Supports 500+ brokers and prop firms including ThinkorSwim, NinjaTrader, Interactive Brokers, Webull, TradeStation, and more
3. **Manual Entry** — Manually enter trades with full execution details. Also supports a Generic CSV format for brokers not yet integrated

## Dashboard

The dashboard is the main hub of TradeZella. It provides a real-time overview of your trading performance.

### Upper Section Widgets (up to 5)
Quick-glance KPIs including: Net P&L, Trade Win %, Profit Factor, Average Win/Loss, Total Trades, Best/Worst Trade, Consecutive Wins/Losses, Largest Win/Loss, Max Drawdown, Average Drawdown, and more.

### Lower Section Widgets
Detailed analytics widgets including: Calendar (Standard and Advanced), Equity Curve, Cumulative P&L, Daily P&L Bar Chart, Distribution charts, Drawdown chart, Win vs Loss comparison, Zella Score, Progress Tracker, Trades table, and more.

### Dashboard Views
- **Dollars View (Default)** — All stats in dollar amounts
- **Percentage View** — All stats as percentages of account balance
- **R-Multiple View** — Stats relative to initial risk per trade
- **Privacy View** — Hides sensitive balance and P&L data
- **Ticks View** — For futures, shows stats in ticks
- **Pips View** — For forex, shows stats in pips
- **Points View** — For futures, shows stats in points

### Dashboard Templates
Save multiple dashboard layouts and switch between them. Customize which widgets appear and where.

## Key Trading Metrics

### Zella Score
A composite performance score (0–100) combining six weighted metrics:
- Profit Factor (25%)
- Average Win/Loss Ratio (20%)
- Maximum Drawdown (20%)
- Trade Win % (15%)
- Recovery Factor (10%)
- Consistency Score (10%)

### MAE (Maximum Adverse Excursion)
The largest unrealized loss during a trade. Shows how far price moved against you before the trade closed.

### MFE (Maximum Favorable Excursion)
The largest unrealized profit during a trade. Shows how far price moved in your favor before the trade closed.

### Zella Scale
Displays potential profit/loss vs. actual profit/loss. Helps identify if you're consistently leaving profits on the table.

### Other Key Metrics
- **Profit Factor** — Ratio of gross profits to gross losses
- **R-Multiple** — Trade result expressed as multiples of initial risk
- **Expectancy** — Average amount you can expect to win/lose per trade
- **Win Rate** — Percentage of trades that are profitable
- **Average Win/Loss** — Average dollar amount of winning vs losing trades
- **Max Drawdown** — Largest peak-to-trough decline
- **Recovery Factor** — Net profit divided by max drawdown
