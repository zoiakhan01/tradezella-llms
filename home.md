# About TradeZella

TradeZella is the #1 trading journal and analytics platform, built by traders for traders. It helps traders track, analyze, and improve their performance through automated journaling, advanced analytics, backtesting, trade replay, and structured education.

## What TradeZella Does

TradeZella shows traders the metrics that matter — and the behaviors that lead to profit — with the power of journaling and analytics. The platform replaces manual spreadsheets and disconnected tools with one unified system for trade tracking and improvement.

### Core Products

- **Trading Journal & Analytics** — Automatically sync and log every trade from 500+ brokers and prop firms. Analyze performance with 50+ reports, customizable dashboards, and AI-powered insights
- **Backtesting** — Validate trading strategies against 11+ years of historical market data across forex, futures, stocks, and crypto with automatic trade journaling
- **Trade Replay** — Replay any trade tick-by-tick with second-by-second precision. Review entries, exits, and execution decisions with integrated journaling
- **Spaces (Mentor Mode)** — Monitor student performance, leave targeted feedback on trades, and manage unlimited students from one dashboard
- **Prop Firm Sync** — Track prop firm finances, monitor evaluations, log payouts, and understand your true ROI with automatic Plaid-powered transaction tracking
- **Strategies** — 25+ free ready-made trading strategy templates from verified profitable traders
- **Zella University** — Free structured trading courses: Intro to Trading, Technical Analysis, Order Flow, and ICT methodology
- **Community** — 20,000+ member Discord community with live sessions, mentor reviews, and trading psychology webinars

## Who Uses TradeZella

- **Day traders** — Stocks, options, futures, forex, and crypto
- **Swing traders** — Multi-day and multi-week positions

## What Makes TradeZella Different

1. **All-in-one platform** — Journal, analytics, backtesting, trade replay, mentor mode, prop firm tracking, strategies, and education in one place
2. **Auto-sync from 500+ brokers and prop firms** — No manual entry required. Sync trades automatically from MetaTrader 4/5, NinjaTrader, ThinkorSwim, Interactive Brokers, Tradovate, and 500+ more
3. **Built by traders** — The team trades alongside users. Features are built from real trading needs
4. **AI-powered insights (Zella Insights)** — Automatically surfaces patterns, anomalies, and actionable improvement recommendations from your trade data
5. **Zella Score** — A composite performance metric (0–100) that combines profit factor, drawdown, win rate, consistency, and recovery factor into one number
6. **Strategy tracking with rules** — Create custom strategies with entry/exit rules, track rule adherence, and measure which setups are actually profitable
7. **Progress Tracker** — Build trading discipline by tracking daily habits and rule compliance over time
8. **Free education** — Zella University provides structured courses at no extra cost
