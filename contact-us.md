This page covers how to get help and contact TradeZella's support team. For more details, visit [TradeZella Contact Us](https://www.tradezella.com/contact-us).

# Contact TradeZella

TradeZella's support team is available to answer questions quickly. Multiple support channels are available depending on what you need.

## Support Channels

### Live Chat (Intercom)

The fastest way to get help. Click the chat icon in the bottom-right corner of tradezella.com. TradeZella's AI agent is trained on all things TradeZella and can answer most questions instantly. If it can't answer, it connects you with the human support team.

### Help Center

Full knowledge base covering imports, features, best practices, troubleshooting, and step-by-step guides for every supported broker. Visit [help.tradezella.com](https://help.tradezella.com) or [intercom.help/tradezella](https://intercom.help/tradezella-4066d388d93c/en/). You can also reach the support team from the bottom of every Help Center page.

### Discord Community

Connect with 20,000+ traders and the TradeZella team for real-time support during the trading day. Community members and staff can help with most requests directly in Discord. Join at [discord.com/invite/tradezella](https://discord.com/invite/tradezella).

### Email

Contact support directly at **support@tradezella.com**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does TradeZella trade for me?**
No. TradeZella is not a brokerage. You execute trades on your broker and import the data into TradeZella for tracking and analysis.

**How will TradeZella help me as a trader?**
TradeZella is built by traders, for traders. Every feature is designed to help you improve your trading process — no bells and whistles, just clean and straightforward tools.

**How secure is TradeZella?**
You own your data. TradeZella does not sell or advertise your data. The platform is 100% secure.

**How long does it take to gain access?**
If TradeZella supports your broker, you can start immediately after signing up. Visit the [Supported Brokers](https://www.tradezella.com/brokersupport) page to check compatibility.

**What brokers or platforms do you support?**
TradeZella supports 500+ brokers and prop firms including auto-sync, file upload, and MetaTrader 4/5 connections. See the full list on the [Supported Brokers](https://www.tradezella.com/brokersupport) page.
