# Zella University

Zella University is TradeZella's free education hub — structured trading courses available to all TradeZella subscribers at no additional cost.

## Overview

Zella University provides a structured curriculum for traders at all levels. Courses are organized into categories and delivered as video lessons with clear learning objectives.

## Course Categories

### Introduction to Trading
Foundational course covering market basics, trading terminology, order types, position sizing, risk management fundamentals, and getting started with TradeZella.

**Lessons include:**
- What is Trading? (5 min)
- Understanding Market Structure (8 min)
- Order Types Explained (6 min)
- Introduction to Risk Management (7 min)
- and more

### Technical Analysis
Deep dive into chart patterns, support/resistance, trend analysis, candlestick patterns, and indicator-based strategies.

**Lessons include:**
- Support and Resistance (10 min)
- Trend Lines and Channels (8 min)
- Candlestick Patterns (12 min)
- Chart Patterns (15 min)
- Moving Averages (9 min)
- RSI and Momentum (8 min)
- and more

### Order Flow
Advanced concepts in reading order flow, volume profile, market microstructure, and tape reading.

**Lessons include:**
- Introduction to Order Flow (10 min)
- Volume Profile Basics (12 min)
- Reading the Tape (8 min)
- Footprint Charts (11 min)
- Delta and Cumulative Delta (9 min)
- and more

### ICT Methodology
Inner Circle Trader (ICT) concepts including fair value gaps, order blocks, liquidity, and smart money concepts.

**Lessons include:**
- ICT Fundamentals (12 min)
- Fair Value Gaps (FVG) (10 min)
- Order Blocks (11 min)
- Liquidity Concepts (9 min)
- Market Structure Shift (8 min)
- ICT Kill Zones (7 min)
- and more

## How to Access

Zella University is available from the main TradeZella navigation menu under "University." All courses are included with both Essential and Pro subscriptions.

## Key Benefits

- **Free** — Included with all TradeZella subscriptions
- **Structured** — Organized curriculum from beginner to advanced
- **Video-based** — Clear, concise video lessons
- **Practical** — Lessons designed to be applied immediately in your trading
- **Integrated** — Learn and practice in the same platform with backtesting and journaling
