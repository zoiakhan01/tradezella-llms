# Zella AI — Your AI Trading Partner

Zella AI is the AI trading partner built into TradeZella. It does the journaling work traders used to do manually: tagging every trade, reviewing every session, generating pre-market plans, and answering questions about your actual trading data. It is not a chatbot. It takes action on your data.

Other trading tools bolt an AI chat window onto a journal. Zella was built the other way around. The AI came first. Your journal is how Zella stores context: every trade, every note, every pattern. The coach came first. The memory keeps it sharp.

Zella AI does not predict the market. It does not tell you what to buy or sell. It does not place trades. It helps you trade better by analyzing your own data, finding patterns in your performance, flagging when you drift from your plan, and surfacing insights you would miss on your own.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Ask Zella — Conversational AI with Memory

Ask Zella AI any question about your trading data in plain language. It already has every trade you have logged, every rule you have set, every note you have written. No screenshots, no copy-pasting, no re-explaining.

**Example questions:**
- "Why do I keep losing on Fridays?"

- "What is my win rate after a 9 AM loss?"
- "Which Strategy lost the most money this month?"

Zella answers with your actual numbers, not generic advice. It pushes back when your data contradicts your assumptions. Every conversation makes the next one sharper.

**Memory:** Zella remembers your name, risk limits, Strategies, and preferences across every session. Month 1, it knows your basics. Month 6, it knows your behavioral patterns. Month 12, it has a full year of your trading history to reference.

### 2. AI Agents — Autonomous Work Without Prompting

Agents are automated workflows that run in the background without you asking. They handle the repetitive analysis and preparation that most traders skip.

**Sentiment Agent (SA-01)**
Knows what the market is doing before you sit down. Auto-detects your assets and scans conditions pre-session. Flags economic events, news, key levels, and volatility regime. Runs every morning in Start My Day with zero input needed.

**Auto-Tagging Agent (AT-02)**
Every trade tagged with zero manual work. Identifies setup type and strategy automatically. Custom label rules written in plain English. Retroactive tagging applied on import. Example: "tag by sector," "tag trades above 2R," "tag revenge trades."

**Session Review Agent (SR-03)**
Every session reviewed. Runs the moment you close your last trade with no input needed. Scores plan adherence and flags broken rules with timestamps. Feeds insights into tomorrow's Pre-Market Prep. Patterns, mistakes, and what to fix tomorrow.

**Backtesting Agent (BT-04) — Coming Soon**
Describe your strategy in plain English. Zella parses rules and runs historical simulation. Results plus Zella AI analysis in seconds. No code, no config.

**Custom Agent (CA-05) — Coming Soon**
Build any agent in plain English. Describe what you want, set triggers, conditions, and actions. Save, share, pause, or edit anytime. No code required. Example: "Flag any trade within 5 minutes of a loss as a revenge trade."

## Daily Workflow — Coached from Open to Close

Zella guides you through three stages every trading day:

### Stage 1: Plan (Pre-Market)
Zella scans your assets, pulls market sentiment, flags economic events, and writes AI recommendations before you sit down. The Market Sentiment Briefing generates a personalized pre-market plan based on your instruments, your trading style, and what you look at. Not a generic news feed. Your plan, based on your configuration.

### Stage 2: Trade (In-Session)
Zella checks every execution against your plan and flags when behavior drifts. Notes and screenshots log automatically. Behavioral nudges alert you when patterns emerge: "You are 3 trades in with no break. Your win rate drops 40% after trade 3."

### Stage 3: Analyze (Post-Session)
Zella tags your trades, analyzes screenshots, flags patterns, and generates insights that feed into tomorrow's game plan. The Session Review scores plan adherence, identifies what went right and what broke your rules, and gives specific recommendations for the next session. Every day, the loop gets tighter. Day 2 starts smarter than Day 1.
