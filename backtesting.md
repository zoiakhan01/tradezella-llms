## Three Ways to Backtest

TradeZella offers three backtesting modes in one platform. No code required for any of them.

### Guided Scenarios (For Finding Your Edge)

No strategy yet? Pick a setup and TradeZella pre-fills the symbol and dates, then points you to where it forms across past sessions, so you learn what to look for before you risk anything.

- We tell you what to test: each scenario describes the setup in plain words, so you are never staring at a blank chart.
- We tell you where to look: jump to the sessions where the setup actually formed and replay them one by one.
- You build the rep: practice the same setup across months of data until you can spot it cold.

### Manual Bar Replay (For Testing Execution)

Step through real history candle by candle, placing live orders as price moves. Practice the execution an automated run cannot teach.

- Dual-timeframe replay: step your execution timeframe and a higher timeframe in sync, so you keep HTF context while you trade the entry.
- Market, limit, and stop orders: place the same order types you trade live. Fills are modeled bar by bar against real historical prices.
- Risk-based PT and SL: set take-profit and stop in R or percentage of balance. Position size is calculated for you so every trade risks the same.
- Auto break-even and controls: move stops to break-even automatically, and step, play, or skip candles at any speed you like.

### Automated Backtesting (For Testing Rules)

Already have rules? Describe them in plain English and the engine runs them across 11+ years of data. Powered by Zella AI.

- Describe it in plain English: set the basics (symbol, timeframe, dates, balance, session), then describe entries, exits, and risk the way you would explain them to another trader.
- Zella AI parses and runs it: Zella AI reads ICT and price-action language like SMT, CHoCH, FVG, PO3, OTE, and ADR. It turns your words into named, structured rules you can read back, names the session, and runs it across the full history.
- Full report and trade log: win rate, profit factor, net P&L, risk metrics, and a written verdict, backed by a trade log you can open trade by trade.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is backtesting?**
Backtesting is the process of testing a trading strategy against historical market data to see how it would have performed. TradeZella offers three modes: guided AI scenarios, manual bar-by-bar replay, and fully automated runs that process years of data in minutes.

**What is automated backtesting?**
Automated backtesting runs your entire strategy across months or years of historical data without you clicking through each bar. TradeZella's engine parses your plain-English rules, simulates every entry and exit, and delivers a full performance report including win rate, profit factor, drawdown, and a chart of every trade.

**How is AI backtesting different from bar replay?**
Bar replay requires you to step through each candle manually, which can take hours per test. Automated backtesting processes the full history at once, the same rules, every bar, all results in minutes, so you can iterate faster and test more variations.

**Can I backtest a strategy without coding?**
Yes. Just describe your rules in plain English (for example, "enter long when the 5m CHoCH forms above a 4h FVG, stop below the FVG, target the 1.5R"). Zella AI parses the logic, shows you the rules it extracted so you can confirm them, then runs the test.

**How accurate is it?**
Every fill is modeled using the actual historical bid/ask data for the instrument. You can review each individual trade on an interactive chart with the entry, stop, and target plotted. The parsed rules are shown before the run so you know exactly what was tested.

**What markets can I backtest?**
Futures, stocks, forex, and crypto. Historical data goes back to September 2014, over 11 years. More instruments and data sources are added regularly.

**Can I backtest manually as well as automatically?**
Yes. Both modes live inside the same workflow. Describe a strategy and run it automatically, or switch to manual replay to step through any session bar by bar. No code required for either.

**Does it understand concepts like FVG, CHoCH, or SMT?**
Yes. The AI natively understands Smart Money concepts including SMT divergence, CHoCH, FVG, iFVG, Power of Three (PO3), OTE, ADR, Asian range, and ATR-based rules. If you use these terms in your strategy description, they are parsed correctly.

**Is TradeZella better than Forex Tester or TradingView for backtesting?**
TradeZella is the only backtesting software that combines no-code automated backtesting in plain English, manual bar replay, guided scenarios, automatic trade journaling, 500+ broker integrations, built-in ICT indicators, Zella AI edge analysis, and 50+ analytics reports in one platform. Forex Tester is forex-only with no journaling. TradingView requires Pine Script and has no journaling.

**Can I share backtesting sessions with my mentor or students?**
Yes. TradeZella's session sharing lets you share backtesting sessions with mentors, students, or trading partners. You can also use Spaces (Mentor Mode) to share your full journal for targeted feedback.
