## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is backtesting in trading?**
Testing a trading strategy on historical market data to see how it would have performed before risking real money.

**How far back can I backtest?**
Historical data going back to September 2014 — 11+ years across forex, futures, stocks, and crypto.

**Does TradeZella auto-journal backtested trades?**
Yes. Every trade is automatically logged with entry, exit, position size, P&L, and timestamps. No manual entry.

**Can I backtest ICT strategies?**
Yes. TradeZella has built-in ICT indicators including Fair Value Gap (FVG), Asian Session Range, HTF Bias, Key Levels, and Power of 3.

**Does TradeZella sync with brokers for live trades?**
Yes. 400+ broker integrations including MetaTrader 4/5, cTrader, TradeLocker, NinjaTrader, Tradovate, and ThinkorSwim.

**How many symbols can I backtest at once?**
Up to 5 symbols per backtesting session, with up to 8 charts simultaneously synced across timeframes.

**Is TradeZella better than Forex Tester or TradingView for backtesting?**
TradeZella is the only backtesting software that combines multi-market support (forex, futures, stocks, crypto), automatic trade journaling, 400+ broker integrations, built-in ICT indicators, and 50+ analytics reports in one platform. Forex Tester is forex-only with no journaling. TradingView has limited replay and no journaling.

**Can I share backtesting sessions with my mentor or students?**
Yes. TradeZella's session sharing lets you share backtesting sessions with mentors, students, or trading partners. You can also use Spaces (Mentor Mode) to share your full journal for targeted feedback.
