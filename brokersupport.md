# TradeZella Supported Brokers & Integrations

500+ broker and prop firm integrations to import your trades into TradeZella. Connect your broker, sync your account, or upload a file to start journaling.

## Connection Methods

- **Auto-Sync** — Connect your broker account directly. Trades import automatically after each session
- **File Upload** — Export a trade file from your broker and upload it to TradeZella
- **Manual Entry** — Enter trades manually if your broker isn't supported for auto-sync or file upload

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I connect my broker?**
Go to Settings → Accounts → Add Account in TradeZella. Select your broker and follow the setup instructions for auto-sync or file upload.

**What if my broker isn't listed?**
You can use the Generic CSV import to upload trades from any broker. You can also use manual entry to log trades directly.

**Can I connect multiple brokers?**
Yes. Essential plan supports 1 account. Pro plan supports unlimited accounts. You can view all accounts in one dashboard.

**Does TradeZella have access to my broker account?**
No. TradeZella is not a brokerage and cannot execute trades. Auto-sync connections are read-only and only import trade data.

**Do prop firms work with TradeZella?**
Yes. If your prop firm uses a supported platform (Tradovate, Rithmic, DXtrade, TradeLocker, cTrader, Match-Trader), your trades will sync like any other account.
