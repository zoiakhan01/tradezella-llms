## Regular Sessions

Structured recurring events to build your trading skills.

- **Pre-Market Prep & Market Breakdowns** — Daily (Monday–Friday) at 4:00 PM. Comprehensive market analysis, key levels, and strategic planning. 30 minutes
- **Backtesting Sessions** — Every other Thursday at 4:00 PM. Live backtesting with strategy breakdowns across all markets. 60 minutes
- **Mentor Mode Sessions** — Every other Thursday at 4:00 PM. Submit your trades and receive personalized feedback from expert mentors. 60 minutes

## How to Join

The TradeZella community is a free Discord server open to all traders.

1. Visit [discord.gg/tradezella](https://discord.gg/RbKYK3pge8) or join from inside your TradeZella account
2. Subscribe to the [event calendar](https://www.addevent.com/calendar/p2hrv4r3zxtj) to get reminders for all upcoming sessions

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is the community free?**
Yes. The Discord community is free and open to all traders, whether or not you have a TradeZella subscription.

**Do I need a TradeZella account to join?**
No. Anyone can join the Discord community. However, a TradeZella subscription is needed to use features discussed in sessions (journaling, backtesting, trade replay, etc.).

**Are sessions recorded?**
Yes. All live sessions are recorded and available to rewatch in the Past Recordings library on the community page.

**How often are events held?**
Pre-market prep runs daily Monday–Friday. Backtesting and Mentor Mode sessions run every other Thursday. Additional special events and webinars are scheduled regularly.
