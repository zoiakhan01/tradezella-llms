## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does TradeZella trade for me?**
No. TradeZella is NOT a brokerage. You execute trades on your broker and then transfer the data into TradeZella to track and analyze your trading performance.

**How will TradeZella help me as a trader?**
TradeZella is built by traders, for traders. Every feature is designed around the question "will this help a trader improve their process?" No bells and whistles — clean and straightforward.

**How secure is TradeZella?**
Data is important to us. TradeZella does not sell or advertise your data, and we are 100% secure. You own your data.

**How long does it take to gain access to TradeZella?**
If we support your broker, you can gain access right away.
