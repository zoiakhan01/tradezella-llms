# TradeZella Spaces (Mentor Mode)

Trading mentor software that gives coaches real-time visibility into every student's performance. Monitor, compare, and provide targeted feedback from one master dashboard — no more relying on screenshots or self-reporting.

## For Mentors

### Real-Time Monitoring

Track student performance with full data access — not screenshots or vague updates.

- **Multi-Student Dashboard** — Track up to 5 students simultaneously with color-coded P&L charts. Compare performance, win rates, and key stats side-by-side
- **Full Account Access** — Enter a view-only version of any student's TradeZella account. Review their Dashboard, Trade Log, Notebook, strategies, and Reports with full context
- **Historical Data Access** — Review a student's complete trading history from the day they joined. Track long-term improvement over weeks, months, or years

### Student Management

- **Unlimited Student Capacity** — No limits on how many students you can invite. Manage 5 or 500 with no per-seat fees
- **Quick Invite** — Send invites via email or shareable link for easy onboarding
- **Full Control** — Remove students or cancel pending invitations instantly with one click

### Targeted Feedback

- **Comment on Trades** — Leave feedback directly on journal entries so comments are attached to the specific trade
- **Notebook Folders** — Create organized coaching folders for structured feedback
- **Feedback That Sticks** — Comments live inside the student's journal, right next to the trade. No more lost Discord messages

## For Students

Students can invite mentors to review their TradeZella account and provide personalized feedback based on real trading data.

- **Invite a Mentor** — Send an invite via email or shareable link. Mentors receive a request they can accept or decline
- **Privacy Controls** — Choose which accounts to share with your mentor. Control the level of access and revoke anytime with one click
- **Full or View-Only Access** — Decide whether your mentor gets full access or view-only access to your account
- **Receive Feedback** — See mentor comments directly on your trades and in Notebook Folders

## How It Works

1. **Invite** — Send an invite via email or shareable link. Students receive a request they can accept or decline
2. **Access** — Student performance appears on the Mentor Dashboard. Click "Play" to enter a view-only version of their account
3. **Review & Feedback** — Review trades, leave comments, and provide targeted feedback exactly where it matters

## Who Uses Mentor Mode?

- **Trading Educators** — Manage cohorts of students. Monitor class-wide performance. Identify who's thriving and who needs support
- **1-on-1 Coaches** — Deep dive into a single trader's account. Review every trade with full context. Build accountability with real data
- **Trading Groups** — Track your entire community's performance from one dashboard. Compare results and foster transparency

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is Mentor Mode?**
Mentor Mode lets trading coaches monitor students' actual performance — real data from synced broker accounts, not screenshots. Mentors can view dashboards, trade logs, and reports, then leave feedback directly on specific trades.

**How do I invite students or connect with a mentor?**
Mentors invite students via email or shareable link. Students can invite mentors the same way, or accept incoming requests from their dashboard. Both parties need active TradeZella subscriptions.

**Can students control what data their mentor sees?**
Yes. Students choose which accounts to share, control the level of access, and can revoke a mentor's access anytime with one click.

**How many students can a mentor track?**
Unlimited. Compare up to 5 students simultaneously on one P&L chart, or click into any student's full account for deeper review.

**Can mentors leave feedback directly on trades?**
Yes. Mentors comment directly on journal entries so feedback is attached to the specific trade.

**Is Mentor Mode included in all TradeZella plans?**
Mentor Mode is available on all paid plans with no per-seat fees. Both mentor and student need active subscriptions to connect.
